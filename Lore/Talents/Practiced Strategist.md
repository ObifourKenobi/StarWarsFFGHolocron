Once per session during a mass combat or combat encounter, the character may take the Practiced Strategist action, making an opposed Knowledge (Warfare) versus Knowledge (Warfare) check targeting the leader of the enemy forces. If the check succeeds, the character (or player) reveals a narrative advantage provided by their strategic planning, such as the timely arrival of reinforcements, sabotage of the enemy's equipment, or a spy planted within the enemy ranks. The exact nature of the revelation is ultimately at the GM's discretion, but it should provide the character and their allies with a significant advantage in the encounter or session.
