The character may make an #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty Knowledge (Education) check when at a dry dock with suitable personnel and equipment to conduct repairs to a starship, or to add attachments or modifications to one. If successful, the cost and time for repairs is reduced by 20% for each rank of Dockyard Expertise to a minimum of 100 credits and one day.
