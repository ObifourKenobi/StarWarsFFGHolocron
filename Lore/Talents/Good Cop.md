The character may spend two #StarWarsAdvantage from a Charm or Negotiation check to upgrade the ability of a single ally's subsequent social interaction skill check against the same target in the same encounter. Upgrade the ability a number of times equal to the character's ranks in Good Cop. A single check may only benefit from one use of Good Cop.
