Force talent. Whenever the character misses an opponent with a combat check and generates a #StarWarsTriumph result or three #StarWarsAdvantage, the character may spend this to perform a Force power action that targets one or more allies as a maneuver (**they** must still be able to perform maneuvers, and may not perform more than two maneuvers in a turn).
