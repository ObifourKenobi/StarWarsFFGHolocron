The character removes a #StarWarsSetback per rank of Street Smarts from [their] Streetwise and Knowledge (Underworld) checks.

Improved
Activation: Active (Action)
Ranked: No
Trees: Investigator, Skip Tracer


Once per session, the character may perform the Improved Street Smarts action. [They] make a #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty  #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty Streetwise or Knowledge (Underworld) check, reducing the difficulty once per rank of Street Smarts. If successful, the GM must reveal one vital clue pertaining to a current mystery the character is attempting to solve.


The clue could be something that the character could not normally find out, but does not have to be the full answer to the mystery (it should be something that cancels a false lead and otherwise helps move the story along). The GM should tailor the information depending on the skill used; Streetwise may mean the character learns about the information from an ad hoc network of street urchins, while Knowledge (Underworld) may mean the character draws on [their] own vast knowledge about criminal enterprise to discover a previously unseen clue.
