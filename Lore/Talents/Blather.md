The character may take the Blather action, making a #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty  Charm or Deception check. If the check is successful, one adversary per success result within medium range is disoriented for a number of rounds equal to the character's [[Presence]] rating. The character may spend a #StarWarsTriumph result from the check to stagger one affected target for one round.
