The character may take the Outmaneuver action, making a #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty  Knowledge (Warfare) check. If the check succeeds, the character chooses one enemy ship or vehicle in the current encounter for each success result. Until the end of the character's next turn, the chosen ships must perform one extra maneuver in order to move or change range bands relative to the character's ship or allies.
