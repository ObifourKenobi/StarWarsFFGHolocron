The character may voluntarily increase the #StarWarsDifficulty of a Gunnery combat check once to inflict a hindering shot on a vehicle. If [they] succeed and deal damage to the target vehicle's hull trauma threshold, the vehicle suffers system strain equal to its current speed whenever it moves until the end of the encounter.
