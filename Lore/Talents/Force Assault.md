Force talent. Upon missing an opponent with a Lightsaber (Willpower) combat check, the character may spend a #StarWarsTriumph result or three #StarWarsAdvantage to perform a Move Force power action as a maneuver this turn (the character must still be able to perform maneuvers and still may not perform more than two maneuvers in a turn).


The character must have already purchased the Move Force power to use it as part of this ability.
