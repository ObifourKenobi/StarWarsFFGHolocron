The character and each ally within short range may spend an #StarWarsAdvantage on their failed combat checks to inflict 1 strain on their respective targets per rank of Suppressing Fire. Each character can only activate this effect once per round.

The following is a homebrew expansion of this talent from a community-produced homebrew sourcebook!

Improved
Activation: Passive
Ranked: No
Trees: Heavy (reSpec)


Foes who suffer strain from the effects of the character's Suppressing Fire and foes within short range of the target character upgrade the difficulty of their next check once.
