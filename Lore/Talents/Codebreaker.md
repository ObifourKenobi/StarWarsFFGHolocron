---
activation: Passive
ranked: Yes

The character removes a setback die per rank of Codebreaker from [their] attempts to break codes or decrypt communications. In addition, the character decreases the difficulty of [their] Computers or Intellect checks made to break codes or decrypt communications by one (this does not increase with additional ranks of Codebreaker).
