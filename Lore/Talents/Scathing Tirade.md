The character may take the Scathing Tirade action by making an #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty Coercion check. For each success result, one enemy within short range suffers 1 strain. The character can also spend #StarWarsAdvantage; for every advantage spent, one target already affected suffers 1 additional strain.

Improved
Activation: Passive
Ranked: No
Trees: Agitator, Politico, Separatist Commander


Each enemy affected by Scathing Tirade suffers a #StarWarsSetback on all skill checks for a number of rounds equal to the character's ranks in Coercion; this does not stack with itself.

Supreme
Activation: Active (Incidental)
Ranked: No
Trees: Agitator, Politico, Separatist Commander


The character may voluntarily suffer 1 strain to perform Scathing Tirade as a maneuver instead of an action.
