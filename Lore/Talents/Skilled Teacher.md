---
activation: Active (Incidental, Out of Turn)
ranked: Yes
---

Before an ally at short range makes a skill check, if that ally has fewer ranks in that skill than the character does, the character may choose to immediately perform a Skilled Teacher incidental to suffer a number of strain, then add an equal number of success results to the ally's next check. The number of strain suffered cannot exceed the character's ranks in Skilled Teacher.
