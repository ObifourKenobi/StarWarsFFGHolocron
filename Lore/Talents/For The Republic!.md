---
activation: Active (Incidental, Out of Turn)
ranked: No
---

Once per encounter when an ally is incapacitated or killed, the character may perform the For the Republic! incidental. The character makes a Daunting difficulty Leadership check. If the check succeeds, the ally is not incapacitated or killed until the end of the following round, instead. If the character's wounds or strain are reduced below their threshold before the end of the next round, they are not incapacitated.
