Force talent. When targeted by a combat check made by an enemy within medium range, the character may spend a Destiny Point to upgrade the difficulty of the check a number of times equal to **their** ranks of Coercion. A character who has purchased this talent automatically gains 1 Conflict at the beginning of each session.
