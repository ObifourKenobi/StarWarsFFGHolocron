When attempting to defend a computer system against intrusion (or when an opponent attempts to slice a computer owned or programmed by the character), the character adds a setback die per rank of Defensive Slicing to [their] opponent's checks.

Improved
Activation: Passive
Ranked: No
Trees: Slicer


When attempting to defend a computer system against intrusion, the character upgrades the difficulty of an opponent's dice pool a number of times equal to [their] rank of Defensive Slicing. This replaces the usual benefits from Defensive Slicing.
