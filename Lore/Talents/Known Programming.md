Once per session, the character may choose a single model of droid that they have encountered before and make a #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty  Vigilance check. If the check is successful, minion and rival droids of that type upgrade the difficulty of their checks targeting the character, plus one ally per success result after the first, once for the rest of the encounter.

Improved
Activation: Passive

Trees: Clone Veteran


Allies affected by Known Programming also upgrade the ability of their checks targeting the chosen droid model once.
