The character removes a #StarWarsSetback per rank of Conditioned from their Athletics and Coordination checks. They reduce the damage and strain suffered from falling by 1 per rank of Conditioned.

While the Force and Destiny Roleplaying Game: Core Rulebook refers to "per rank of Coordination", the Errata (Page 1) corrects this to "per rank of Conditioned".  

The following is a homebrew expansion of this talent from a community-produced homebrew sourcebook!

Improved
Activation: Active (Incidental)
Ranked: No
Trees: Archaeologist (reSpec)


Once per session while falling, the character may spend 1 Destiny point to take the Improved Conditioned Incidental. They reduce range bands fallen by ranks of Conditioned, to a minimum of Short. In the case of the Game Master requiring the character to fall a certain distance, their initial, damaging impact is the n ew range band, after which they have a controlled, non-damaging descent.
