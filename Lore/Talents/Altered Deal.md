Once per session the character may take the Altered Deal action and make #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty  Coercion check to radically change a previously agreed upon deal or bargain to a version much more advantageous to the character. The player must narratively explain what happened, including the details of how the character arranged for the deal to be changed as well as the specifics of the new deal.
