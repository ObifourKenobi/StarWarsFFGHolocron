After rolling the dice pool for a Mechanics or Computers check but before interpreting the results, the character may voluntarily suffer a number of strain no greater than ranks in Eye for Detail to convert that many success results into #StarWarsAdvantage. (The character must still have at least one success in the results at the end to succeed on the check.)
