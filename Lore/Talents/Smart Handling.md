Once per session, while aboard a starship of silhouette 4 or higher, the character may take the Smart Handling action; making a #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty  Knowledge (Education) check. If the check is successful, until the start of the next round, the ship's handling increases by two plus one per #StarWarsAdvantage scored on the check to a maximum handling of +4. A #StarWarsTriumph result can be spent to extend the effect until the end of the encounter.
