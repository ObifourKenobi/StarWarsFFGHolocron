activation: Active (Incidental)
ranked: No

Once per round while piloting a starship or vehicle, the character may suffer 2 strain to showboat during **their** next check. If the check succeeds, the character adds a #StarWarsTriumph result to the results. However, if the check fails, **they** add a despair instead. The #StarWarsTriumph or #StarWarsDespairs added this way do not include the success or failure results that are usually part of them, only the beneficial or negative effects of the symbol.
