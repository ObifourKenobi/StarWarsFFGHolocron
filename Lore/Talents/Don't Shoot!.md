Once per session, the character can take the Don't Shoot! action to make a Hard difficulty Charm check. If [they] succeed, [they] cannot be the target of a combat check until the end of the encounter or until [they] make a combat check. This player must explain how [they] talk [their] way out of being associated with [their] party's combatants, and the GM has final say on whether [their] ruse succeeds.
