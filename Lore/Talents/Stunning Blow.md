When making Melee checks, the character may choose to deal damage as strain instead of wounds. This does not ignore soak.

Improved
Activation: Active (Incidental)
Ranked: No
Trees: Gadgeteer, Infiltrator


When dealing strain damage with Melee or Brawl checks, the character may spend a triumph result to stagger [their] target for one round per triumph spent.
