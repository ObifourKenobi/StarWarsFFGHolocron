When the character performs the Gain the Advantage action, a number of allied vehicles equal to **their** ranks in Leadership and within close range also gain the benefits of the action on the target. These allied vehicles maintain the benefits until they leave close range with the character or the character loses the benefits of Gain the Advantage.
