Force talent. The character may take the Saber Throw action, making a Lightsaber combat check as a ranged attack at one target within medium range, adding #StarWarsForce no greater than their Force rating to the check. The character must spend a Force point and succeed on the check to hit their target; they may spend a Force point to have their weapon return to their hand after resolving the attack.
