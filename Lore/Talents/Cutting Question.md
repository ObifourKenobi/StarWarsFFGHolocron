Once per encounter, the character may make one Coercion skill check using **their** Deception skill. The character's Deception skill is combined with **their** [[Willpower]] characteristic to form the dice pool. When the character takes the Cutting Question incidental, the player must explain how, in this particular instance, **their** Deception skill is helping to erode **their** foe's resolve.
