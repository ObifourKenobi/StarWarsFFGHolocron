Force talent. After making a crafting check to create a potion or talisman, the character may choose to suffer strain no greater than twice their ranks in Alchemical Arts. For every two strain, they may add a light point or dark point to the results. If they choose to add a dark point, they also suffer 1 Conflict per dark point.
