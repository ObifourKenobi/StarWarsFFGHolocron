When using weapons with the Blast quality or explosive devices, the character may spend an #StarWarsAdvantage to exclude one target that would normally be affected by the explosion. Multiple targets may be excluded in this manner, but the total number may not exceed **their** ranks in Selective Detonation. The character may also spend a #StarWarsTriumph result to exclude multiple targets, as long as the total number of targets excluded does not exceed **their** ranks in Selective Detonation.
