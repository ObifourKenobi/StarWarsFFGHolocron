Upon making an unsuccessful attack with a starship or vehicle weapon, the character may spend two #StarWarsAdvantage per rank of Overwhelm Defenses. Reduce the defense rating in the defense zone targeted by the attack of the remainder of the encounter by 1 for every two #StarWarsAdvantage spent.
