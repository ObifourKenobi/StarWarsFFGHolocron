When piloting a starship or vehicle, the character may take a Corellian Sendoff action targeting two enemy starships or vehicles at close range that are both of equal or smaller silhouette to the character's ship. The character makes a #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty  Cool check; if it succeeds, the targeted ships immediately suffer a minor collision with each other.
