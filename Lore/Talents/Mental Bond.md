Force talent. The character may take the Mental Bond action, committing a #StarWarsForce. As long as a #StarWarsForce remains committed, [they] may communicate mentally with an animal bonded to [them] via the Animal Bond talent. [They] can see and hear what it sees and hears, and in structured encounters, [they] may direct [their] animal at up to extreme range.
