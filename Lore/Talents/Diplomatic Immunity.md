---
activation: Active (Incidental, Out of Turn)
ranked: No
---

Once per encounter, the character may perform the Diplomatic Immunity incidental. The character spends one Destiny Point, and adversaries cannot choose the character as the target of a combat check until after the end of the character's next turn. This effect ends if the character makes a combat check.
