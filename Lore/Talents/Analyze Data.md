ranked: No

Once per session, after capturing or stealing data about an opponent, the character may perform the Analyze Data action to make an Average difficulty Knowledge (Warfare) check to determine its value. If successful, once before the end of the session the character may add an automatic success results equal to their ranks in Knowledge (Warfare) to one check related to that captured data. The GM has the final say on what checks are related to the data.
