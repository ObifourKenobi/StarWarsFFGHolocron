The character may spend two #StarWarsAdvantage from a Deception or Coercion check to upgrade the ability of a single ally's subsequent social interaction skill check against the same target in the same encounter. Upgrade the ability a number of times equal to the character's ranks in Bad Cop. A single check may only benefit from one use of Bad Cop.
