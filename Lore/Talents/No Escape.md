Force talent. When making a Coercion check, the character may spend two #StarWarsAdvantage to cause one enemy within short range to lose **their** free maneuver during **their** next turn. When a foe within short range makes a Fear check, the character may spend two threat results from that check to cause that enemy to lose **their** free maneuver during **their** next turn.
