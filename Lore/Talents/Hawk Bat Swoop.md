Force talent. The character may take a Hawk Bat Swoop action, performing a Lightsaber (Agility) melee combat check against one target within short range and adding #StarWarsForce no greater than Force rating to the pool. The character may spend a Force point before resolving the success or failure of the check to engage the target immediately as an incidental, and may spend Force points to add #StarWarsAdvantage to the check. If the character cannot move to engage the target, the attack automatically misses.
