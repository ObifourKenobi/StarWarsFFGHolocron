If a character is carrying items that total 2 encumbrance or less (after factoring in reductions such as for armor being worn), at the end of the encounter [they] heal all strain that [they] are currently suffering.


If a character normally carries items that total more than 2 encumbrance but discards or otherwise loses those items temporarily, the GM can rule that the character still does not gain the benefits of Iron Soul.
