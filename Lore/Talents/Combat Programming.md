Once per encounter when working on a droid, the character may make a #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty  Computers check. If successful, for the remainder of the encounter the droid gains two different combat skills at rank 1, or 1 additional rank in two different existing combat skills (to a maximum of 5), as chosen by the character. If the character is a droid, it may perform this action on itself.
