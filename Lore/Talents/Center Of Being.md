Force talent. Once per round, when wielding a lightsaber, the character may perform a Center of Being maneuver. Until the beginning of the character's next turn, whenever an enemy makes a melee attack targeting the character, the critical rating of the enemy's weapon counts as 1 higher per rank of Center of Being.

Improved
Activation: Passive
Ranked: No
Trees: Master, Niman Disciple


Force talent. The character may voluntarily suffer 1 strain to perform Center of Being as an incidental instead of a maneuver.

Supreme
Activation: Passive
Ranked: No
Trees: Master


Force talent. When the character performs the Center of Being maneuver, until the beginning of their next turn, ranged weapons also treat their critical rating as one higher per rank of Center of Being when attacking the character.
