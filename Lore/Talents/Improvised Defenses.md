The character may attempt an #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty Survival check to fashion small defenses using scavenged materials. If the check is successful, the structure can provide cover for up to 4 characters for the rest of the encounter. The character may spend two #StarWarsAdvantage or a triumph result from the check to increase the ranged defense the structure provides to 2.
