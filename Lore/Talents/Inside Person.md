Once per session, the character may spend a Destiny Point to establish [they] have previously been undercover gathering information in an identified enemy base or large vehicle. For the remainder of the session, add automatic two #StarWarsAdvantage to checks [they] or [their] allies make that are associated with that locations (such as checks to attack it or social skill checks targeting personnel in it).
