The character gains a #StarWarsBoost per rank of Command when making Leadership checks (or other checks to inspire, lead, or rally an audience). Inspired targets also add a #StarWarsBoost per rank to any subsequent Discipline checks they make over the next twenty-four hours (this does not increase with additional ranks of Command).
