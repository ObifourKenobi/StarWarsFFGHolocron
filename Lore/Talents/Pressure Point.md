When making a Brawl check against a living opponent, the character may choose to forgo dealing damage as wounds, instead dealing the equivalent damage as strain, plus additional strain equal to **their** ranks in Medicine. These checks cannot be made with any weapons, but this strain damage is not reduced by soak.
