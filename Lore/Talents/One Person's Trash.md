The character may make a Perception check in order to locate a particular item amid a battlefield, shipwreck, junkyard, or other location with potential salvage. The difficulty of the check is equal to half the item's rarity, rounded down. There is no cost to acquire an item this way, but the item is in need of moderate repairs when found.
