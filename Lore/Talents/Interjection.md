After another character makes a social check, the character may suffer 3 strain and make an #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty Vigilance check. The character may add a success result or failure result to the results of the chosen check equal to the success results on the Vigilance check and an #StarWarsAdvantage or threat result equal to #StarWarsAdvantage on the Vigilance check.
