Once per encounter, after the character has rolled a skill check (but before resolving the result), [they] choose a number of positive dice from [their] dice pool up to [their] ranks in Second Chances and re-rolls them. The character must accept the second result. This talent cannot be used to re-roll any #StarWarsForce.
