Once per game session, the character may leap forward to create a daring distraction on the battlefield by performing the Seize the Initiative maneuver. [They] make a Hard difficulty Athletics check. If [they] succeed, any number of other PCs who have yet not acted this round may immediately take their turns as if there were that many PC slots at that point in the Initiative order. Each PC who acts this way counts as having acted for this round (and thus cannot act again this round. The Initiative order returns to its previous order at the beginning of the next round.
