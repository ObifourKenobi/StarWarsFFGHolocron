Force talent. Once per session, the character may perform the Power of Darkness maneuver. If they do so, they increase their wound threshold and strain threshold by 1 per Dark Side Destiny point currently in the Destiny pool until the end of the encounter. A character who has purchased this talent automatically gains 1 Conflict at the beginning of a game session.
