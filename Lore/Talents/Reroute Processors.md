Once per encounter, the character may perform the Reroute Processors action on a droid **they** are engaged with by making an #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty Computers check. If successful, the character decreases on of the droid's characteristics by one (to a minimum of 0) until the end of the encounter and increases another of its characteristics by one (to a maximum of 7) until the end of the encounter. If the character is a droid, it may perform this action on itself.
