Force talent. Once per session, when making a single check, the character may treat **their** Force rating as being equal to **their** ranks in Knowledge (Lore).

While the Force and Destiny Roleplaying Game: Core Rulebook description for this talent begins with, "Force power", it is confirmed to be a "Force talent" by Table 4-1 as well as the two Mystic trees on which it appears.
