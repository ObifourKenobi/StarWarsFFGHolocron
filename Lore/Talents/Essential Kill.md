Force talent. When making a combat check that does not involve either using the Gunnery skill or operating a non-starship weapon, the character may add #StarWarsForce no greater than [their] Force rating to the check. The character may spend a Force point (◐) to add an #StarWarsAdvantage, or three Force points (◐◐◐) to add a triumph result (character's choice), to the result. #StarWarsAdvantage gained in this way cannot be used to recover strain.
