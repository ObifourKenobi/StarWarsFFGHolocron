Each rank of More Machine than Man increases the character's cybernetic implant cap by 1.

The following is a homebrew expansion of this talent from a community-produced homebrew sourcebook!

Improved
Activation: Active (Maneuver)
Ranked: No
Trees: Cyber Tech (reSpec)


Before making a check, the character may perform the Improved More Machine Than Man maneuver, suffer 1 strain, and add #StarWarsBoost to the next check equal to half the number of the character's installed cybernetics, rounded up. The player must specify how one or more of their cybernetics are aiding them for this check.
