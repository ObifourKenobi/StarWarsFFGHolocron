---
activation: Active (Maneuver)
ranked: No
---

When in a starship or vehicle, the character can make a Fire Control maneuver. If [they] do so, all combat checks from the starship or vehicle count the silhouette of the target as one higher than normal until the beginning of the character's next turn. This effect does not stack with multiple uses of the Fire Control maneuver.
