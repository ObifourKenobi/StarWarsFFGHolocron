Force talent. When piloting a vehicle of silhouette 5 or smaller, the character may take the Intuitive Evasion maneuver, suffering 1 strain and committing a number of #StarWarsForce no greater than **their** Force rating or **their** ranks of Intuitive Evasion. **they** then upgrade the difficulty of combat checks targeting **their** vehicle by an equal amount. The character suffers 1 strain at the beginning of each of **their** turns in which these dice remain committed.
