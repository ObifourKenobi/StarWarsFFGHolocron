Before making a check using an item, the character may take the Exceed Specifications incidental, adding a #StarWarsBoost to the check. The GM may spend two threat results on the check to damage the item one step, or a despair to damage it beyond usability. Threat or #StarWarsDespairs may have additional consequences depending on the nature of the item, at the GM's discretion.

Improved
Activation: Passive
Ranked: No
Trees: Scavenger


When the character takes the Exceed Specifications incidental, upgrade the ability of the check once instead of adding a #StarWarsBoost.

Supreme
Activation: Active (Incidental)
Ranked: No
Trees: Scavenger


If the character's check benefitting from the Exceed Specifications incidental generates a #StarWarsTriumph result, they may damage the item one step to roll one additional #StarWarsProficiency and add it to the check results. If the check generates a #StarWarsDespair, roll a challenge die and add it to the results.
