While wearing armor, the character increases their total soak value by one.

Improved
Activation: Passive
Ranked: No
Trees: Armorer, Clone Trooper, Commando, Death Watch Warrior, Gadgeteer


When wearing armor with a soak value of two or higher, the character increases their defense by one.

Supreme
Activation: Active (Incidental, Out of Turn)
Ranked: No
Trees: Armorer


Once per round, when the character suffers a Critical Injury, **they** may suffer 3 strain to take the Armor Master incidental. If **they** do, **they** reduce the Critical Injury result that **they** suffer by 10 per point of **their** soak, to a minimum of 1.
