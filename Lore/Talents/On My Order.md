The character may take the On My Order action, making a #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty  Leadership check. If the check succeeds, the character chooses a number of crew aboard their ship equal to their [[Presence]]. Those crew members upgrade the ability of their next Gunnery check once, plus one time for every additional two success results on the check.
