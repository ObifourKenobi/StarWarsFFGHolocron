When targeted by an opposed social skill check, the character may suffer 2 strain to perform the Resist Questioning out-of-turn incidental, adding a #StarWarsSetback to the check. If the check fails, they may spend a #StarWarsDespair to indicate they have provided false or misleading information that the target believes to be truthful.

Improved
Activation: Passive
Ranked: No
Trees: Interrogator


When one ally within short range is targeted by an opposed social skill check, the character may perform Resist Questioning to affect this check instead. If the check fails, **they** may spend a despair to indicate the ally has provided false or misleading information that the target believes to be truthful.
