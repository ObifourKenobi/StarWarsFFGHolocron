Once per session, if the character's Signature Vehicle would be destroyed, the character may spend a Destiny Point to save it. If it would be destroyed by a Critical Hit, the Critical Hit is still suffered, but the effect is ignored. If it would be destroyed by some other event, the details of how the starship or vehicle survives the near-destruction are up to the player and the GM.
