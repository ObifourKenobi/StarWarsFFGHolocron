Once per game session, the character may identify one bunker, weapon emplacement, or other fixed combat structure (subject to the GM's approval) and then take a Weak Foundation action; making a #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty  Knowledge (Warfare) check. If the character succeeds, until the end of the encounter the character and all allies add two automatic #StarWarsAdvantage or a success result to all combat checks made targeting the structure (active character's choice).
