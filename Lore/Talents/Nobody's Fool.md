The difficulty of any Charm, Coercion, and Deception checks attempted against the character is upgraded once for each rank of Nobody's Fool.

Improved
Activation: Active (Incidental, Out of Turn)
Ranked: No
Trees: Master, Senator


When targeted by a Charm, Coercion, or Deception check, the character may spend a #StarWarsDespair or three threat results from the check to inflict strain on the opponent equal to the character's ranks in Nobody's Fool.
