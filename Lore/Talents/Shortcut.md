During a chase, the character adds a #StarWarsBoost per rank of Shortcut to [their] checks made to catch or escape an opponent.

Improved
Activation: Active (Incidental)
Ranked: No
Trees: Navigator, Operator, Racer


When engaging in a chase or race, the character may suffer two strain to add success results equal to ranks in Shortcut to the check.

While Savage Spirits: A Sourcebook for Seekers shows the Improved talent as "Activation: Passive", the Errata (Page 3) corrects this to "Activation: Active (Incidental)".
