When resolving an attack from a personal (non starship/vehicle) explosive or ordnance weapon, the character may spend an advantage or triumph result to have the weapon's Blast quality affect all characters within short range (rather than engaged). If the weapon normally affects all characters within short range, then the range of effect is increased to medium instead.
