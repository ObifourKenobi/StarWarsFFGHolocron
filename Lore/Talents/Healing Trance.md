Force talent. The character may commit a #StarWarsForce. For every full encounter a #StarWarsForce remains committed, the character heals 1 wound [they] are suffering per rank of Healing Trance. This is in addition to wounds healed due to natural rest or other abilities. If in a situation without defined encounters, the GM can have Healing Trance take effect every 12 hours.

Improved
Activation: Passive
Ranked: No
Trees: Healer, Magus


Force talent. Whenever the character heals wounds as a result of Healing Trance, if the character is suffering from a Critical Injury, [they] make a Resilience check with the difficulty equal to the Critical Injury's severity rating. On a successful check, the character recovers from the Critical Injury. If the character is suffering from multiple Critical Injuries, [they] choose which one to attempt to recover from.
