Once per session, the character may make a #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty  Knowledge check (the GM and player should determine which Knowledge skill is most applicable in the given situation) to perform the Thorough Assessment action. If **they** succeed, the character may add a #StarWarsBoost to one check that another character who could reasonably benefit from this information makes before the end of the encounter. For every success beyond the first, **they** may add a #StarWarsBoost to one additional check this way. **they** cannot allocate more than one #StarWarsBoost to any single check this way. At the end of the encounter, any unused #StarWarsBoost are lost.
