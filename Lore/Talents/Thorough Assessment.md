Once per session, the character may make a Hard difficulty Knowledge check (the GM and player should determine which Knowledge skill is most applicable in the given situation) to perform the Thorough Assessment action. If [they] succeed, the character may add a boost die to one check that another character who could reasonably benefit from this information makes before the end of the encounter. For every success beyond the first, [they] may add a boost die to one additional check this way. [They] cannot allocate more than one boost die to any single check this way. At the end of the encounter, any unused #StarWarsBoost are lost.
