The character may perform the Parry incidental while unarmed. When the character performs the Parry incidental while unarmed, reduce the strain they suffer from the Parry incidental by one, to a minimum of 1.

The following is a homebrew expansion of this talent from a community-produced homebrew sourcebook!

Improved
Activation: Passive
Ranked: No
Trees: Martial Artist (reSpec)


Whenever an engaged enemy makes a combat check, after the attack is resolved, the character may spend three threat or a #StarWarsDespairs to disarm the attack, and may equip the used weapon. If not equipping the weapon, it falls within short range at a location of the player's choice.
