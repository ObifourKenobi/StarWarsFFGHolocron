Once per session the character may take the targeted Firepower action by identifying one detected enemy character, vehicle, starship, or structure and make a Hard difficulty Knowledge (Warfare) check. If successful, for the remainder of the encounter all allies within short range of the character add an automatic #StarWarsAdvantage to their combat attacks against that target equal to success results that the character gained in the check.
