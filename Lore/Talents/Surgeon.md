When this character makes a Medicine check to help a character heal wounds, the target heals one additional wound per rank of Surgeon.

The following is a homebrew expansion of this talent from a community-produced homebrew sourcebook!

Improved
Activation: Active (Incidental)
Ranked: No
Trees: Doctor (reSpec)


When performing a Medicine check, suffer strain up to their ranks of Surgeon, then choose either to remove that many #StarWarsSetback or add that many #StarWarsBoost to the check.
