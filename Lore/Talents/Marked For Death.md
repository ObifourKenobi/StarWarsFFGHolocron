Force talent. The character may take the Marked for Death maneuver, selecting one target and committing a Force die. The character adds two automatic #StarWarsAdvantage to combat checks against that target while a Force die remains committed, but cannot use the Marked for Death talent again until the character uncommits #StarWarsForce, the original target is incapacitated, or the session ends. #StarWarsAdvantage gained in this way cannot be used to recover strain.
