Once per game session, when an ally is about to suffer a critical injury, the character can take an It's Not That Bad incidental to make a #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty  Medicine check. If successful, the ally does not gain the Critical Injury and does not suffer any of its normal effects (although the attack that caused the Critical Injury still deals damage as normal).
