Force talent. After being exposed to any unknown substance, as an out of turn incidental, the character may roll #StarWarsForce no greater than their Force rating. They may spend two Force points to immediately identify the composition and any effects of the substance.


At the GM's discretion, this might provide a #StarWarsBoost to the character's later attempts to recreate this substance.
