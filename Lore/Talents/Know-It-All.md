Once per session, the character may, as if [they] had spent a Destiny Point, perfectly recall one important fact [they] previously learned. If the character's controlling player does not remember the specifics of the fact the player wishes to have the character recall, the Game Master should remind [them] of the relevant facts (provided the character has actually encountered the information).
