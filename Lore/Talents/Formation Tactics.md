The character may take the Formation Tactics action by making a #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty  Leadership check. If successful, they may choose a number of allies within short range equal to success results generated. The difficulty of attacks against these allies is upgraded once until the end of the character's next turn. The effects of this action do not stack.

Improved 
Activation: Active (Maneuver)
Ranked: No
Trees: Clone Commander, Imperial Academy Cadet


The difficulty of taking the Formation Tactics action is reduced to Average instead of Hard. The character may also spend a #StarWarsTriumph result or six #StarWarsAdvantage to have the effect last until end of encounter.
