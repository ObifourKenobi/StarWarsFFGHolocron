_Force talent._ As a maneuver, may suffer 3 strain to give the currently equipped weapon the following item qualities until the end of the next round: [Defensive](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Item_Qualities#Defensive_\(Passive\) "Item Qualities") 1, [Deflection](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Item_Qualities#Deflection_\(Passive\) "Item Qualities") 1. Weapons that already have these qualities increase their existing ratings by 1.
