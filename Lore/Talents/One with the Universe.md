_Force talent._ Once per session, the character may spend several minutes meditating, then take the One with the Universe action, making an **[Average difficulty](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Narrative_Dice#Standard_Difficulties "Narrative Dice") [Astrogation check](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Astrogation_\(Int\) "Astrogation (Int)")**. If successful, during the next encounter **they** add a [Light point](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Narrative_Dice#Light_Point "Narrative Dice") to all Force power checks. However, if **they** succeeded with a #StarWarsThreat(https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Narrative_Dice#Threat "Narrative Dice") result, **they** add a [Dark point](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Narrative_Dice#Dark_Point "Narrative Dice") to all Force power checks instead.
