Force talent. When making a check to repair or craft a non-starship or vehicle item, the character may add #StarWarsForce no greater than Force rating to the check. The character may spend two Force points to permanently increase the number of hard points the item has by 1, to a maximum of 2 additional hardpoints. An item may only be improved in this way once.
