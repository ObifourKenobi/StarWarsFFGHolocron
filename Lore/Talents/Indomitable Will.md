Force talent. Once per encounter, the character may perform the Indomitable Will maneuver, suffering 3 strain to commit a number of #StarWarsForce up to their Force rating. While #StarWarsForce remain committed, reduce all damage the character suffers by an amount equal to the committed #StarWarsForce, and the character suffers 1 strain at the beginning of each of their turns.
