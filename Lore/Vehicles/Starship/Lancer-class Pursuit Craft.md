## Description

Borrowing liberally from Corellian Engineering Corporation's design language and philosophy, the *Lancer*-class rides the line between civilian freighter and warship.

## Weapons

- Dorsal turret mounted triple light laser cannon (Fire Arc All Dorsal; Damage 5; Critical 3; Range [Close]; Linked 2)
- Forward-mounted twin light laser cannon (Fire Arc Forward; Damage 5; Critical 3; Range [Close]; Linked 1)
- Ventral-mounted light tractor beam projector (Fire Arc All Ventral; Damage –; Critical –; Range [Close]; Tractor 2)

## Special Rules

**Mk IV FCS:** As a maneuver, the pilot may activate or deactivate the Mk. IV FCS. While the system is active, the ship's forward-mounted twin light laser cannons' Linked item quality increases to 4, and it gains the Tractor 2 item quality, but only this weapon system can be fired.
