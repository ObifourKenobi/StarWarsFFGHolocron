## Description

Developed for the Republic Navy during the waning days of the Clone Wars, Slayn & Korpil's H-60 Tempest Heavy Bomber is a now uncommon but well-respected heavy strike fighter.

## Weapons

- Forward-mounted medium laser cannons (Fire Arc Forward; Damage 6; Critical 3; Range [Close]; Linked 3)
- Forward-mounted twin light ion cannons (Fire Arc Forward; Damage 5; Critical 4; Range [Close]; Ion, Linked 1)
- Forward-mounted proton torpedo launcher (Fire Arc Forward; Damage 8; Critical 2; Range [Short]; Blast 6, Breach 6, Guided 2, Limited Ammo 12, Slow-Firing 1)
- Ventral-mounted proton bomb bay—can only be used against surface targets or capital ships (Fire Arc Down; Damage 7; Critical 2; Range [Close]; Blast 7, Breach 8, Limited Ammo 12)
