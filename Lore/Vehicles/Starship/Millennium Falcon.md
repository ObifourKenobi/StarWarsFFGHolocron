## Description

Certainly, the most famous YT-1300 in existence, the *Millennium Falcon* is the ship that made the Kessel Run in less than twelve parsecs, the ship that spirited the Death Star plans to the Rebels' Massassi base, and the ship crewed by Heroes of Yavin.

## Weapons

- One dorsal and one ventral turret-mounted heavy laser cannon (Fire Arc All; Damage 6; Critical 3; Range [Short])

## Additional Rules (Lando's Falcon)

**Calrissian Chronicles:** A character may add two boost dice to Charm and Deception checks they make to leverage the *Falcon*'s reputation as the transport of famous adventurer Lando Calrissian, but their targets add an automatic success result to checks they make to subsequently identify or track down the character.

## Additional Rules (Han Solo's Falcon)

**Hunk of Junk:** Once per session, a character may remove a despair result from a Piloting or Mechanics check they make using the *Millennium Falcon*. The GM may then add a despair result to a subsequent roll any character makes using the *Millennium Falcon* that session.
