## Description

The Alpha-3 *Nimbus*, known colloquially as the V-wing is the Republic Navy's newest starfighter.

## Weapons

- Port and starboard wing-mounted light laser cannons (Fire Arc Forward; Damage 5; Critical 3; Range [Close]; Linked 3)
- Forward-mounted concussion missile launchers (Fire Arc Forward; Damage 6; Critical 3; Range [Short]; Blast 4, Breach 4, Guided 3, Limited Ammo 4, Slow-Firing 1)

## Syluiure-45 Hyperspace Docking Ring

Designed by TransGalMeg in association with KSE, the Syliure-45 is a medium range hyperspace sled designed to provide hyperspace capabilities to both the Eta-2 Actis and the Alpha-3 Nimbus interceptors. The successor to the Syliure-31 used by the previous generation Delta-series fighters, the Syliure-45 is a relatively simple affair consisting of six power ion engines and a KSE Mk.II Jumpmaster Class 1 hyperdrive.
