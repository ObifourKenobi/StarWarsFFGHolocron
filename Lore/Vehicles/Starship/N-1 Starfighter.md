## Description

The starfighter used by Naboo's royal spacefighter corps for escort and recon was as luxurious as every other vessel used by the royal family. Featuring a chromium finish and sleek design iconic to Nubia Star Drives, the final product was manufactured by Theed Palace Space Vessel Engineering Corps.

No expense was spared in the design of this nimble and well equipped starfighter. Not only did it feature an exceptional hyperdrive, powerful engines, powerful medium laser cannons and proton torpedo tubes; all with a powerful shielding system to boot… it also paid attention to the ecological impact of the vessel.
