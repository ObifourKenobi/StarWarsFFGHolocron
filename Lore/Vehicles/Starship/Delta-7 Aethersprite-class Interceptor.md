## Description

Small, fast, and deadly, Kuat Systems Engineering's Delta-7 *Aethersprite*-class light interceptor is one of the newest additions to the Republic's vast arsenal.

## Weapons

- Forward-mounted twin medium laser cannons (Fire Arc Forward; Damage 6; Critical 3; Range [Close]; Linked 1)

## Syliure-31 Hyperspace Docking Ring

Consisting of two powerful ion engines and a medium-range Class 1 hyperdrive, it gives the Delta-series fighters respectable hyperspace capabilities. Docking with a hyperspace ring takes an **Easy difficulty Piloting (Space check)** as an action.
