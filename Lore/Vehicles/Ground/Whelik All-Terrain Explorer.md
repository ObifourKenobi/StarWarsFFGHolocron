## Description

Designed and produced by Corellian Mining Corporation to fill the need for a civilian vehicle capable of penetrating the most dense, inhospitable terrain, the Whelik has six high-arching legs that give the vehicle its distinctive silhouette while at the same time making it ideal for accessing rugged, nearly impossible-to-reach locations on remote planets.
