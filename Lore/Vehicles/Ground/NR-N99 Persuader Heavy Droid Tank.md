## Description

The NR-N99 is among the CIS military's deadliest combat vehicles. It is a droid vehicle that operates without a crew and can attack with weapons and perform pilot-only actions simultaneously.

## Weapons

- **Port and starboard light ion cannons** (Fire Arc: Forward; Damage: 5; Critical: 4; Range: Close; Ion, Linked 1)
- **Port and starboard shock cannons** (Fire Arc: Forward; Damage: 8; Critical: 3; Range: Close; Blast 6, Linked 1, Slow-Firing 1, Vicious 2)
- **Forward-mounted heavy repeating blasters** (Fire Arc: Forward; Damage: 15; Critical: 2; Range: Long; Auto-fire, Pierce 2, Vicious 1) - Uses personal scale
- **Forward-mounted flex ordnance launchers** (Fire Arc: Forward; Damage: 6; Critical: 3; Range: Short; Blast 4, Breach 4, Guided 3, Limited Ammo 6, Linked 1, Slow-Firing 1)
