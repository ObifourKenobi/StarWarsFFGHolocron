## Description

A lesser-known variant of the 610-AvA Dart, the Dash speeder bike is just as easy to use but also features a collapsible frame for easy storage and transportation.

## Weapons

None

## Special Rules

**Collapsible:** Collapsing or expanding the Dash requires a maneuver. When collapsed, the vehicle is silhouette 1 and cannot be used.
