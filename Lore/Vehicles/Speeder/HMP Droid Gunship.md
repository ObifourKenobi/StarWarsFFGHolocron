## Description

The Heavy Missile Platform (HMP) droid gunship is an automated airspeeder used by the Separatist Alliance during the Clone Wars. It is heavily armed and designed for ground attack missions.

## Weapons

- **Two medium laser cannons** (Fire Arc Forward; Damage 6; Critical 3; Range [Close]; Linked 1)
- **Two missile launchers** (Fire Arc Forward; Damage 6; Critical 3; Range [Short]; Blast 4, Breach 1, Guided 2, Limited Ammo 8)
- **Turret-mounted light laser cannon** (Fire Arc All; Damage 5; Critical 4; Range [Close])
