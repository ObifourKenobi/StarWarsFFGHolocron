## Description

The Low Altitude Assault Transport/infantry (LAAT/i) is a military gunship used by the Grand Army of the Republic during the Clone Wars for troop deployment and close air support.

## Weapons

- **Two dorsal-mounted mass driver missile launchers** (Fire Arc Forward; Damage 6; Critical 3; Range [Short]; Blast 4, Breach 1, Guided 2, Limited Ammo 6)
- **Two chin-mounted laser cannons** (Fire Arc Forward; Damage 7; Critical 3; Range [Close]; Linked 1)
- **Two door-mounted repeating blasters** (Fire Arc Port/Starboard; Damage 6; Critical 3; Range [Close]; Auto-fire)
