## Description

The Bloodhound models by SoroSuub are medium-sized landspeeders built for professional planetary survey teams. The Bloodhound is equipped with SoroSuub's PVA.q LRTS, a powerful scanning suite designed to render three-dimensional maps of surrounding terrain.
