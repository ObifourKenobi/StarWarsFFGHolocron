Pretty much anything to do with technology or mechanical devices lies within the Technician's purview.

# Specializations
## Mechanic
Virtually no Alliance military unit can function without a competent Mechanic, due to the constant wear-and-tear on the mechanical and technical assets the unit relies upon to do battle. // Depending on who is asked, Mechanics are either the glue that holds everything together or the grease that makes it all go.
**Engineer Career Skills**: [[Athletics]], [[Computers]], [[Education]], [[Mechanics]], [[Perception]], [[Piloting-Space]], [[Ranged-Light]], [[Vigilance]]
