# Jedi

Before the Clone Wars, the Jedi acted as an independent troubleshooting body and also as a monastic order meditating on the nature of the Force and philosophizing on members' role in the galaxy. Now, they serve as generals and commanders constantly involved in war.

> The Jedi begin the game with a Force rating of 1.
