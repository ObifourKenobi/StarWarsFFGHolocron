There would be no Galactic Civil War if not for the massive technological capabilities of both the Empire and the Alliance.
# Specializations
## Mechanic
Virtually no Alliance military unit can function without a competent Mechanic, due to the constant wear-and-tear on the mechanical and technical assets the unit relies upon to do battle.
**Mechanic Bonus Career Skills**: [[Brawl]], [[Mechanics]], [[Piloting-Space]], [[Skulduggery]]

**20 XP**: [[Contraption]], [[Solid Repairs]], [[Fine Tuning]], [[Hard Headed]]
**25 XP**: [[Natural Tinkerer]], [[Hold Together]], [[Dedication]], [[Hard Headed|Improved Hard Headed]]
