This skill covers the amount of knowledge a character knows about the worlds of the galactic Core, such as Coruscant and Corellia. It may be used in a roll such as;

- Identifying a person's planet of origin from appearance and mannerisms.
- When interacting with a member of the Core Worlds, as to not come off as offensive.
- Recognizing the imports, exports and illegal goods of the Core Worlds.
Careers with Knowledge (Core Worlds) as a class skill
- Colonist
- Diplomat
- Sentinel
Specializations with Knowledge (Core Worlds) as a class skill
- Ambassador
- Clone Commander
- Figurehead
- Master
- Politico
- Trader
Universal Specializations with Knowledge (Core Worlds) as a class skill
- Republic Representative (can choose any one knowledge skill)
Talents which turn Knowledge (Core Worlds) into a career skill
- Well Traveled talent
