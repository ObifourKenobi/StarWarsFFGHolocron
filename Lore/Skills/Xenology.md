This skill determines a character's knowledge on the differences between different species, fauna and flora of the galaxy. It may be used in rolls such as;

- Figuring out how to interact with an individual of a different race.
- Helping or harming a subject of a different race.
- Knowing what to offer as sustenance to a different race.
Careers with Knowledge (Xenology) as a class skill
- Diplomat
- Explorer
- Seeker
Specializations with Knowledge (Xenology) as a class skill
- Alchemist
- Arbiter
- Beast Rider
- Big-Game Hunter
- Healer
- Hermit
- Medic
- Scholar
- Survivalist
Universal Specializations with Knowledge (Xenology) as a class skill
