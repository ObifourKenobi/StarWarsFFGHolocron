This social skill judges the character's ability to trick others into believing falsehoods. It is used against Discipline, and may be rolled in situations such as;

- Misleading a buyer on the worth of an item.
- Distracting an opponent.
- Leaving a false trail in a chase.
- Bluffing their way through a tricky situation.
Careers with Deception  as a class skill
- Colonist
- Diplomat
- Sentinel
- Smuggler
- Spy
Specializations with Deception as a class skill
- Advisor
- Advocate
- Agitator
- Courier
- Gambler
- Infiltrator
- Performer
- Politico
- Propagandist
- Prophet
- Scoundrel
- Trader
Universal Specializations with Deception as a class skill
- Pirate
- Senator
