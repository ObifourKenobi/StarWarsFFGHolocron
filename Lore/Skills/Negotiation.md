This social skill represents a character's ability to barter and keep their cool when trading. This skill is used in rolls involving;

- Purchasing goods for any less than the advertised price.
- Selling goods.
- Making an agreement or contract.
- Finding a seller for a good.
Careers with Negotiation  as a class skill
- Colonist
- Consular
- Diplomat
Specializations with Negotiation as a class skill

- Advocate
- Ambassador
- Arbiter
- Charmer
