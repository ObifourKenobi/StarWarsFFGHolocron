## Description

The Mk IV riot armor is one of Creshaldyne Industries' most popular models.

## Special Qualities

This armor includes a breath mask that allows the user to filter out airborne toxins and gases (but not to breathe in atmospheres without the character's required atmosphere mix). In addition, when staggered or disoriented, the wearer reduces the duration of the effect by one round, to a minimum of one round.
