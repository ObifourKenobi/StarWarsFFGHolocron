## Description

Some of the heaviest non-powered personal armor on the market, Steelskin armor is illegal on most worlds and sure to draw significant attention in even the roughest of Outer Rim settlements.

## Special Qualities

All Brawn- and Agility-based checks while wearing Steelskin armor gain a setback die. Whenever the bearer is staggered or disoriented from a weapon's item quality, the armor reduces the rounds staggered or disoriented by one.
