## Description

Popular and lightweight, capacitive armor offers moderate protection against most forms of attack whenever worn.

**Models include:** A/KT Tuff-Powered Armor

## Special Qualities

As a maneuver, the wearer may power up or power down the armor. While the armor is powered and the plates are locked in place, the wearer gains +1 soak and +1 defense but loses their free maneuver during each of their turns.
