## Description

The latest suit in Merr-Sonn's N-series armor for combat engineers has broken with the company's typical policy of selling exclusively to established planetary militaries, and is available for general purchase on the open market.

## Special Qualities

As a maneuver, the wearer may recharge an energy weapon or device that has run out of power or ammunition in the manner of an extra reload/power pack.
