## Description

Chitin armor is usually made by hand from the husks of various giant insect species found throughout the galaxy.

## Special Qualities

Chitin armor makes a strong statement about the wearer and these suits tend to have a startling effect on people in civilized society. A character wearing a suit of chitin armor adds an automatic advantage result to their Coercion checks and an automatic threat result to their Charm checks. The Game Master may determine that the armor provides further benefits or drawbacks, depending the situation.
