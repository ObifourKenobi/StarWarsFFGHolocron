## Description

The elite ARC troopers within the Grand Army of the Republic receive special gear, including a unique type of reinforced armor, to help them survive their dangerous assignments.

## Special Qualities

Phase I ARC trooper armor includes a comlink. The various systems built into Phase I ARC trooper armor remove a setback die from all Perception checks made by the wearer. The armor also provides all the benefits of a hardened comlink. In addition, the integrated load-bearing harness increases the wearer's encumbrance threshold by three.
