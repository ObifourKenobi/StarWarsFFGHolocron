## Description

Advanced Recon Commandos, or ARC troopers, performed the initial field test of Phase II clone trooper armor.

## Special Qualities

Phase II ARC trooper armor includes an integrated comlink with scrambler capable of using all standard Republic communications protocols. The survival pack incorporates emergency rations, a water filtration system, a microfiber line, and a distress beacon.
