## Description

Crafted especially from the Shell Hutts of Circumtore, these massive exoskeletons are fashioned from segmented cylinders of durasteel.

## Special Qualities

Shell armor allows its wearer to hover up to five meters above the ground.

Hutt Shell Armor allows a Hutt to ignore the effects of the Awkward and Ponderous abilities (if they possess either or both of these). Further, the wearer does not have to spend additional maneuvers when navigating difficult terrain.
