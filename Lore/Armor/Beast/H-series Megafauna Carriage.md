## Description

Piccatech's H-series megafauna carriage is an essential piece of kit for those working with oversized creatures.

## Special Qualities

A beast must have a silhouette of at least 3 and a Brawn of at least 5 to be fitted with a howdah. Basic howdah configurations are readily available for beasts with a tetrapodal or quadrupedal stance, like fambaa or dewback, at the listed price. Howdahs for creatures with more appendages, like the six-legged acklay, can be had for a small additional fee, generally 500 credits. Howdahs can be armored and fitted with one or two Gunnery or vehicle-scale weapons, following the usual restrictions for silhouette.

A megafauna carriage can only be equipped to bestial riding animals. In addition to its other effects, using it allows the character to ride the beast without suffering bareback riding penalties.
