## Description

Lighter and more flexible than the heavy Destri-series armor, Capari-series padded beast armor by Piccatech Ltd. offers an excellent balance between protection and wearability.

## Special Qualities

Padded beast armor can only be equipped to bestial riding animals. In addition to its other effects, using it allows the character to ride the beast without suffering bareback riding penalties.
