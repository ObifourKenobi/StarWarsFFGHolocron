## Description

During martial arts training, both students and teachers put themselves at risk as they work through the process of education.

## Special Qualities

The armor gains +1 soak when the wearer suffers damage from Brawl weapons or bludgeoning Melee weapons. A character adds a setback die to any Brawn- or Agility-based checks while wearing this armor.
