## Description

At their most basic, enviro-suits are fully sealed, full-body suits made of sturdy materials designed to keep sentients alive in inhospitable places.

## Special Qualities

Enviro-suits have an operation life of eight hours until they need to be recharged and refilled. Due to their many layers of protective shielding, enviro-suits tend to be bulky and reduce a wearer's sense of their surroundings. An individual wearing an enviro-suit suffers one setback die on Agility- and Perception-based checks.
