## Description

The popular Wing Commander armored flight suit by Ayelixe/Krongbing Textiles is the latest advancement in pilot comfort and protection.

## Special Qualities

Thanks to its rugged construction and integrated armor, the Wing Commander suit reduces any strain received from Critical Hits dealt to the wearer's vehicle by one (to a minimum of 1) and the flame-resistant coating reduces damage dealt to the wearer by fires and weapons with the Burn quality by 1.
