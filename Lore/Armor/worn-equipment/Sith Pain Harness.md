## Description

Developed over generations, the Sith Pain Harness represents the pinnacle of their philosophy.

## Special Qualities

While wearing the Sith Pain Harness, a character can choose to suffer one wound as an incidental to add an automatic success result to the next Discipline check they make during the current round. They then suffer 1 Conflict.
