## Description

The typical Jedi training suit features bulky, heavily padded chest, leg, and arm pieces designed to absorb the stunning blows of training lightsabers.

## Special Qualities

Unless the integrated weights are removed, a Jedi training suit has an encumbrance of 6. Removal of these weights, which requires an Easy difficulty Mechanics check, reduces the suit's encumbrance to 4. At the GM's discretion, training while wearing the weights might help a character develop physical fitness or adapt to high-gravity environments.
