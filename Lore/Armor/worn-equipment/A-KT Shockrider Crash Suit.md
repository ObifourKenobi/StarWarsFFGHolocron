## Description

Specially designed for those who make their living behind the wheel, the Shockrider crash suits from Ayelixe/Krongbing Textiles Corporation are built to help pilots of speeders, speeder bikes, swoops, and other fast atmospheric vehicles survive even the worst crashes.

## Special Qualities

Thanks to its rugged construction and integrated armor, a crash suit reduces any strain received from Critical Hits dealt to the wearer's vehicle by 1 (to a minimum of 1) and the flame-resistant coating reduces the damage dealt to the wearer by fires and weapons with the Burn quality by 1.
