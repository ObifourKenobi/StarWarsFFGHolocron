## Description

Rare, expensive, and difficult to use, personal deflector shields are one of the few types of personal armor capable of withstanding sustained blaster fire.

## Special Qualities

When the wielder is using a PDS, the GM can spend any despair result they generate to have it run out of power for the remainder of the encounter.
