## Description

A reflect body glove is a skintight suit that can be worn underneath traditional garments.

## Special Qualities

Add two setback dice to a character's Vigilance and Perception checks to notice the user is wearing armor. After a successful combat check has been resolved against the wearer, the reflect body glove's soak is reduced by one, to a minimum of zero. The suit's soak may be restored to its original value by making an Average difficulty Mechanics check.
