## Description

Worn by welders, machinists, tinkerers, and mechanics throughout the galaxy, fabricators' protective gear is a catchall term for a varied collection of heavy padded clothing.

## Special Qualities

This armor allows the wearer to ignore the effects of fires or acids of rating 1 or 2. It has a soak value of 3 against damage from weapons with the Blast quality, but only when the quality is activated and used to inflict damage on the wearer. It provides only 1 point of soak against all other damage that was not inflicted through that quality.
