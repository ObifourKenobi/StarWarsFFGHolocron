## Description

Beloved by harried quartermasters across the galaxy, hauling harnesses offer convenient means to lift and move heavy loads.

**Models include:** TaggeCo. Mobile Lifting Gear.

## Special Qualities

A character wearing a hauling harness increases their encumbrance capacity by 6, but also upgrades the difficulty of any combat check they make twice.
