## Description

The peculiar brainchild of an exiled Arkanian engineer, the "Gundark" scav-suit is becoming an increasingly common sight across the galactic underworld.

## Special Qualities

The inbuilt sensor array on a "Gundark" scav-suit adds a boost die to the wearer's Perception checks, and an additional boost die to all checks to detect or analyze technological parts and items. However, if the suit's systems become damaged due to a Critical Injury, aimed shot, or other circumstance, the wearer instead suffers a setback die due to sensor feedback on all Perception checks instead until the suit is repaired.
