## Description

Ayelixe/Kronbing's Climbsuit is a popular set of lightweight armor designed for climbing and rappelling.

## Special Qualities

Wearing a Climbsuit upgrades the ability of the wearer's checks to climb or rappel down a surface once. The Climbsuit also reduces damage and strain suffered from falling by 5 and reduces any Critical Injury results from falling by 10.
