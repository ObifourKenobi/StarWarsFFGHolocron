## Description

Similar to helmets worn by civilian engineers and construction workers, an Alliance engineer's helmet is made of sturdy, lightweight, fiber-reinforced polycarbonate and fitted with a shock-absorbing gel liner.

## Special Qualities

While wearing an Alliance engineer's helmet and vest, the character counts as having 1 rank (or one additional rank) in the Durable talent.
