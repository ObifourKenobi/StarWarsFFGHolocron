## Description

Referred to snidely as "bounty hunter insurance" by cynics across the galaxy, the Koromondain armored half-vest is designed to protect the wearer from ambushes and sudden attacks from unexpected directions.

## Special Qualities

Whenever a character wearing an armored half-vest suffers a Critical Injury, the armor becomes damaged by one step.
