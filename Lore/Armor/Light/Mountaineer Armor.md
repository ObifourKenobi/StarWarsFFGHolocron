## Description

Another offering from Ayelixe/Krongbing Textiles, mountaineer armor is a suit of light, flexible armor designed to assist the wearer in climbing and navigating mountainous terrain.

## Special Qualities

Mountaineer armor adds two #StarWarsBoost dice to all [Athletics] checks made by the wearer to climb or rappel.
