## Description

A common piece of attire for well-traveled Corellians, the innocuous catch vest has saved the lives of many free traders. These vests appear to be ordinary clothing but contain energy-dissipating materials.

## Special Qualities

Catch vests have a soak value of 2 against damage from energy-based weapons only; they provide only 1 point of soak against all other forms of damage.
