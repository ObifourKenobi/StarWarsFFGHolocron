## Description

Originally designed by a high-end clothing manufacturer for making literally flashy entrances, the flare jacket caught the attention of organizations that saw potential military use and contracted Ayelixe/Krongbing Textiles to produce a more intense version for a personal defense coat.

**Models include:** Ayelixe/Krongbing Personal Defense Coat, Brennkeyes Syndicate flare jacket.

## Special Qualities

Once per encounter, a character wearing a flare jacket can trigger the charges as a maneuver. Each character within short range (besides the wearer) must make a **Hard difficulty [Vigilance check]**. Each character who fails is staggered for one round, plus one additional round per three [threat] results.
