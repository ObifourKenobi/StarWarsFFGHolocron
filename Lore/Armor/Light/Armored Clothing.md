## Description

Available in a variety of styles, armored clothing is an excellent option for those who need light, flexible protection.

**Models Include:** Ayelixe Fabrico Armorweave, A/KT Tuff1 Combat Jumpsuit, Tenloss Syndicate "ShadowStalker" Assassin Gear.

## Special Qualities

As this armor is reasonably subtle, it takes a thorough search of an individual's person to detect that they're wearing it, an action that requires an Average difficulty Perception check.
