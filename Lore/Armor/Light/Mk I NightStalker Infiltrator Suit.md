## Description

The Mk I NightStalker is a lightweight, surprisingly durable suit of armor that Bantin Corp recently produced. Designed for covert operations, it provides excellent stealth capabilities.

## Special Qualities

When wearing a NightStalker suit, the wearer adds an automatic [success] result to [Stealth] checks they make and adds an automatic [threat] result to social skill checks they make.
