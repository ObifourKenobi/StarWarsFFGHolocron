## Description

In the chaos of a major conflict, many clone troopers fear that the sheer volume of blaster bolts fired from both sides could catch even a Jedi Master unprepared. Jedi Reconnaissance Armor provides lightweight protection for Jedi operating in forward positions.

## Special Qualities

Characters wearing Jedi reconnaissance armor add a #StarWarsBoost die to [[Stealth]] checks they make.
