## Description

The traditional robes of the Jedi are appropriate for their work as mediators and peacekeepers, but when leading detachments of clone troopers, many Jedi have opted for additional protection to fit their new role. Jedi Commander Armor provides enhanced defense while allowing the mobility needed for lightsaber combat.
