## Description

Like most types of armored clothing, second skin armor offers moderate protection while still enabling easy movement and flexibility. Designed to be worn beneath normal clothing.

**Models include:** Preparix Second Skin, Aylixe/Krongbing Undershield.

## Special Qualities

Add two #StarWarsSetback dice to a character's [[Perception]] checks to notice second skin armor on the wearer.
