## Description

Blaster and other advanced weapons can make a mockery of even the most sophisticated armor. Beast-hide armor is crafted from the hides of dangerous creatures, offering basic protection while projecting a primitive, intimidating appearance.

## Special Qualities

When wearing beast-hide armor, a character gains an automatic #StarWarsAdvantage result on [[Coercion]] checks and an automatic #StarWarsThreat on [[Charm]] checks. The GM may determine that this armor provides further benefits or drawbacks depending on the situation.
