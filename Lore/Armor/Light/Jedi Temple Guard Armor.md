## Description

The Jedi Temple Guard were some of the best fighters the Jedi Order had to offer, sworn to protect the temple to the end. Their distinctive armor provides excellent defense while maintaining the anonymity expected of these sacred protectors.

The armored mask contains a helmet comlink and in-helmet scanner.
