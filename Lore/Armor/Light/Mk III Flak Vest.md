## Description

The Mk III flak vest from Pacnorval Defense Systems is a basic jacket consisting of light durasteel plates under layered ballistic fabric. It provides reliable protection against explosions and shrapnel.

## Special Qualities

This armor gains +1 soak when the wearer suffers damage from the Blast quality.
