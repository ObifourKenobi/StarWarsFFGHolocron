## Description

Completely encased in an ebony suit of custom life support armor, Darth Vader cuts a fearsome silhouette and generates an air of mystery; the strained, unnerving gasps of his respiratory implant the only insight into stoic Vader's mood.

## Special Qualities

Darth Vader's customized armor comes with an enhanced optics suite (remove two setback dice from Perception, Vigilance, and combat checks due to environmental effects that obscure vision), an integrated medical system (add a boost die to all Medicine checks to heal Vader; once per session, may activate this attachment as an incidental; this counts as using a stimpack), and are vacuum-sealed (can ignore the effects of vacuum for up to an hour).

**Peerless Armor:** Once per encounter as an out-of-turn incidental, the wearer of Darth Vader's armor may spend a Destiny Point to increase the soak provided by this armor to +6 until the end of the wearer's next turn.

## Legendary Ability

**Visage of Death:** Once per encounter after Darth Vader kills an Imperial character, he may spend a Destiny Point to add a success and advantage result to checks made by other Imperial characters in the current encounter for the remainder of the encounter.
