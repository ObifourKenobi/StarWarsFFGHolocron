## Description

Many ancient Jedi Knights favored heavy concealing robes for their anonymity, and now Force users throughout the galaxy use them to avoid notice. These robes are designed to obscure the wearer's features and identity.

**Models Include:** Various cuts and combinations.

## Special Qualities

- **Concealing**: Thanks to their cut and construction, concealing robes add a setback die to checks to notice or recognize an individual wearing them.
