## Description

One of the more ingenious utilizations of holographic technology comes in the form of wearable tech, commonly used for communications and advertisements. These costumes can project different outfits over the wearer, allowing for quick changes of appearance.

**Models Include:** A/KT "Versatility" Outfit, Plescinia Micro Holo Suit.

## Special Qualities

- **Holographic Disguise**: To record a new outfit, a character must make an **Easy difficulty Computers check** while in possession of a garment to be recorded, or a **Daunting difficulty Computers check** to create a facsimile of a garment that is not on hand.
