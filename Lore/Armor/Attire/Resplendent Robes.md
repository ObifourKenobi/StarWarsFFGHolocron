## Description

Some beings naturally become the center of attention when they enter a room, but dressing for the part never hurts, either.

## Special Qualities

As long as the character wearing resplendent robes purposefully draws attention to themselves, each of their allies in medium range adds a boost die to Perception, Skulduggery, and Stealth checks.
