## Description

Although often inconvenient to wear, severely limiting to movement, and historically archaic, few things command innate respect as much as the traditional attire of a planet's nobility. Noble regalia marks the wearer as someone of importance and breeding.

## Special Qualities

- **Noble Bearing**: The wearer downgrades the difficulty of social checks to interact with other nobility and their subordinates once.
