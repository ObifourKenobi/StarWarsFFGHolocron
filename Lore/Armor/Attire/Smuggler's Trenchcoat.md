## Description

Every smuggler gets searched; that part of the job is inevitable. The smuggler's trenchcoat is designed specifically to help conceal contraband during inspections, with hidden compartments and clever construction.

## Special Qualities

- **Hidden Compartments**: Checks made to find anything hidden within a smuggler's trenchcoat while it is being worn are opposed by the Skulduggery of the wearer. The coat can conceal items with a total encumbrance value of up to 2.
