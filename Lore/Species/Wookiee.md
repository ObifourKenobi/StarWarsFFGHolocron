# Wookiee
## Special Abilities:
Wookiees begin the game with oner rank in [Brawl](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Brawl_\(Br\) "Brawl (Br)"). They still may not train [Brawl](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Brawl_\(Br\) "Brawl (Br)") above rank 2 during character creation.
## Additional information
- **Wookiee Rage:** When a Wookiee has suffered any wounds [they] deal +1 damage to Brawl and Melee attacks. When a Wookiee is Critically Injured, [they] instead deal +2 damage to Brawl and Melee attacks.
