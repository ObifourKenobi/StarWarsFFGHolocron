# Gank
## Special Abilities
Ganks begin the game with one rank in Coercion or Vigilance. They still may not train Coercion or Vigilance above rank 2 during character creation.
## Additional information
- **Cyborg**: All Ganks are cyborgs, and each one often posseses several different cybernetic implants. A Gank begins play with up to two cybernetics that cost a total of up to 5,000 credits. If a character takes on additional Obligation for credits at character creation, [they] may apply these extra funds to the 5,000 credit budget to purchase cybernetics. Ganks have a cybernetic implant cap of 3 plus [[Brawn]] rating.
