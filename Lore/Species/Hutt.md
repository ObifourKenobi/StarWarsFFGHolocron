# Hutt
## Special Abilities:
Hutts begin the game with one rank in the [[Enduring]] and one rank in the [[Nobody's Fool]]. Hutts begin the game with one rank in [[Coercion]] or [[Discipline]]. They still may not train [[Coercion]] or [[Discipline]] above rank 2 during character creation.
## Additional information
- **Ponderous:** A Hutt can never spend more than one maneuver moving per turn.
