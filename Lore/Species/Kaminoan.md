# Kaminoan
## Special Abilities
Kaminoans begin the game with one rank in [[Medicine]]. They still may not train [[Medicine]] above rank 2 during character creation. They also start the game with one rank in the [[Researcher]].
## Additional information
- **Expressionless:** Kaminoans add a #StarWarsSetback die to all [[Charm]] checks they make. Other characters add a #StarWarsSetback die to all Social skill checks they make when targeting Kaminoans.
