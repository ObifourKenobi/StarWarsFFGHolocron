# Kaminoan
## Special Abilities
Kaminoans begin the game with one rank in [Medicine](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Medicine_\(Int\) "Medicine (Int)"). They still may not train [Medicine](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Medicine_\(Int\) "Medicine (Int)") above rank 2 during character creation. They also start the game with one rank in the [Researcher talent](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Researcher_talent "Researcher talent").
## Additional information
- **Expressionless:** Kaminoans add a #StarWarsSetback die to all [Charm](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Charm_\(Pr\) "Charm (Pr)") checks they make. Other characters add a #StarWarsSetback die to all Social skill checks they make when targeting Kaminoans.
