# Falleen
Beneath the glamorous reptilian features and serene facade of the mysterious Falleen lies a shrewd mind and cold-blooded precision.
## Special Abilities
Falleen begin the game with one rank in [Charm](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Charm_\(Pr\) "Charm (Pr)"). They still may not train [Charm](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Charm_\(Pr\) "Charm (Pr)") above rank 2 during character creation.
## Additional information
- **Beguiling Pheromones:** By emitting, pheromones and altering their skin color, Falleen can affect emotional states of other sentients. Once per check as an incidental, a Falleen may suffer 2 strain to upgrade the ability of a [Charm](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Charm_\(Pr\) "Charm (Pr)"), [Deception](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Deception_\(Cun\) "Deception (Cun)"), or [Negotiation](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Negotiation_\(Pr\) "Negotiation (Pr)") check against a living sentient being within short range once. This ability has no effect on targets wearing breath masks or without respiratory systems.
