# Muun
Despite being an Outer Rim society, the Muun remain an important center in galactic politics; they started and maintain the InterGalactic Banking Clan (otherwise known as the Banking Clan or IGBC) and stabilized the credit used from the deepest Core World all the way to the edge of the Outer Rim.
## Special Abilities
Muun begin the game with one rank in [Knowledge (Education)](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Education_\(Int\) "Education (Int)") and one rank in [Knowledge (Core Worlds)](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Core_Worlds_\(Int\) "Core Worlds (Int)"). They still may not train [Knowledge (Education)](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Education_\(Int\) "Education (Int)") or [Knowledge (Core Worlds)](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Core_Worlds_\(Int\) "Core Worlds (Int)") above rank 2 during character creation.
## Additional information
- **Deep Pockets:** Muun characters start the game with an additional 1,000 credits (they may not spend these credits during character creation).
