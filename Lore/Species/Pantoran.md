# Pantoran
These blue-skinned near-humans come from a cold climate and were vibrant participants in galactic society during the time of the Republic.
## Special Abilities
Pantorans begin the game with one rank in either [[Cool]] or [[Negotiation]]. They still may not train [[Cool]] or [[Negotiation]] above rank 2 during character creation.
## Additional information
- **Tundra Dwellers:** When making skill checks, Pantorans may remove a #StarWarsSetback die imposed due to cold environmental conditions.
