# Besalisk
## Special Abilities:
Besalisks begin the game with one rank in [Resilience](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Resilience_\(Br\) "Resilience (Br)"). They still may not train [Resilience](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Resilience_\(Br\) "Resilience (Br)") above rank 2 during character creation.
## Additional information
- **Additional Limbs**: Besalisks have multiple sets of arms. As a result, they gain an additional free maneuver per turn, though still may not perform more than two maneuvers per turn.
