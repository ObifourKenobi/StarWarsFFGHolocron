# Drabatan
Music-obsessed amphibious humanoids, Drabatans come from the freshwater swamps of Pipada.
## Special Abilities
Drabatans begin the game with one rank in either [[Charm]] or [[Leadership]]. They still may not train either skill above rank 2 during character creation.
## Additional information
- **Big Noise:** When making a [[Leadership]] or [[Coercion]] check, Drabatans can spend two #StarWarsAdvantage results to affect one additional character not originally targeted by the check or affect targets at one range band further than normal.
