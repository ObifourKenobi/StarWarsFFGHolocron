# Drabatan
Music-obsessed amphibious humanoids, Drabatans come from the freshwater swamps of Pipada.
## Special Abilities
Drabatans begin the game with one rank in either [Charm](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Charm_\(Pr\) "Charm (Pr)") or [Leadership](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Leadership_\(Pr\) "Leadership (Pr)"). They still may not train either skill above rank 2 during character creation.
## Additional information
- **Big Noise:** When making a [Leadership](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Leadership_\(Pr\) "Leadership (Pr)") or [[Coercion]] check, Drabatans can spend two [advantage](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Narrative_Dice#Advantage "Narrative Dice") results to affect one additional character not originally targeted by the check or affect targets at one range band further than normal.
