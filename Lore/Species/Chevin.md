# Chevin
## Special Abilities:
Chevin begin the game with one rank in [Negotiation](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Negotiation_\(Pr\) "Negotiation (Pr)"). They still may not train [Negotiation](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Negotiation_\(Pr\) "Negotiation (Pr)") above rank 2 during character creation.

## Additional information
- **Advanced Olfaction**: Though it originally developed to track prey, a Chevin's keen sense of smell can be valuable in many situations. Add a [boost](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Narrative_Dice#Boost_\(Blue\) "Narrative Dice") die to [Perception](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Perception_\(Cun\) "Perception (Cun)") checks involving the sense of smell.
- **Thick Hide**: A Chevin's hide is thick and tough enough to absorb some damage, so they start the game with one rank in the [Durable talent](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Durable_talent "Durable talent").
