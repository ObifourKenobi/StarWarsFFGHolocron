# Clawdite
Native to the planet Zolan in the Mid Rim, Clawdites are primarily known across the rest of the galaxy for their innate ability to transform their appearance.
## Special Abilities
Clawdites begin the game with one rank in [Resilience](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Resilience_\(Br\) "Resilience (Br)"). They still may not train [Resilience](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Resilience_\(Br\) "Resilience (Br)") above rank 2 during character creation.
## Additional information
- **Changeling:** As an action, a Clawdite may suffer 3 strain and make an #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty **[Resilience check](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Resilience_\(Br\) "Resilience (Br)")**. If the Clawdite succeeds, [they] change [their] appearance to match that of a silhouette 1 character whom [they] have observed before. An observing character must make opposed **[Perception](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Perception_\(Cun\) "Perception (Cun)") vs [Deception](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Deception_\(Cun\) "Deception (Cun)") check** to detect that something is amiss with the impersonated character's likeness. As always, the GM can add a #StarWarsBoost die or #StarWarsSetback die for situational effects that might affect the check, such as a #StarWarsBoost die if the Clawdite's garb does not match expectations or a #StarWarsSetback die if the Clawdite has studied the impersonated individual's mannerisms closely.
