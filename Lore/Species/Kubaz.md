# Kubaz
Distinguished by their long snout and green-black skin as well as the whirling trill of their native tongue, the Kubaz flit from shadow to shadow.
## Special Abilities
Kubaz begin the game with one free rank in [Stealth](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Stealth_\(Ag\) "Stealth (Ag)") or [Survival](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Survival_\(Cun\) "Survival (Cun)"). They still may not train [Stealth](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Stealth_\(Ag\) "Stealth (Ag)") or [Survival](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Survival_\(Cun\) "Survival (Cun)") above rank 2 during character creation.
## Additional information
- **Enhanced Vision:** When making Ranged combat or [Perception](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Perception_\(Cun\) "Perception (Cun)") checks, Kubaz may remove up to two [setback](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Narrative_Dice#Setback_\(Black\) "Narrative Dice") dice imposed due to concealment or appliable environmental factors (but not defense).
