# Shistavanen
## Special Abilities
Shistavanens begin the game with one rank in either [Brawl](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Brawl_\(Br\) "Brawl (Br)") or [Survival](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Survival_\(Cun\) "Survival (Cun)"). They still may not train [Brawl](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Brawl_\(Br\) "Brawl (Br)") or [Survival](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Survival_\(Cun\) "Survival (Cun)") above rank 2 during character creation.
## Additional information
- **Hunter's Instincts:** When making a check for initiative, a Shistavanen may make a [Survival](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Survival_\(Cun\) "Survival (Cun)") check instead of a [Discipline](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Discipline_\(Will\) "Discipline (Will)") or [Cool](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Cool_\(Pr\) "Cool (Pr)") check.
