# Pau'an
Gaunt, ghoulish, and towering over most other sentient species of the galaxy, Pau'ans are easily recognizable by their intimidating presence.
## Special Abilities
Pau'ans begin the game with one rank in [Negotiation](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Negotiation_\(Pr\) "Negotiation (Pr)") or [[Coercion]]. They still cannot train [Negotiation](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Negotiation_\(Pr\) "Negotiation (Pr)") or [[Coercion]] above rank 2 during character creation.
## Additional information
- **Sensitive Hearing:** Pau'ans add a #StarWarsBoost die to all [Perception](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Perception_\(Cun\) "Perception (Cun)") and [Vigilance](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Vigilance_\(Will\) "Vigilance (Will)") checks they make as long as they are wearing their protective earpieces. If they are not wearing these earpieces, they do not gain the bonus, and they add a #StarWarsThreat result to [Perception](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Perception_\(Cun\) "Perception (Cun)") and [Vigilance](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Vigilance_\(Will\) "Vigilance (Will)") checks they make instead.
