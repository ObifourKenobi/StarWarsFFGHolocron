# Kalleran
- **Special Abilities:** Kallerans begin the game with one rank in [Streetwise](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Streetwise_\(Cun\) "Streetwise (Cun)"). They still may not train [Streetwise](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Streetwise_\(Cun\) "Streetwise (Cun)") above rank 2 during character creation.
- **Hypersensitive Antennae:** Kallerans begin the game with the [Heightened Awareness talent](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Heightened_Awareness_talent "Heightened Awareness talent").
