# Drall
## Special Abilities
Drall begin the game with one rank in [[Education]]. They still may not train [[Education]] above rank 2 during character creation. Drall have a mind for problems and solutions. In addition to using [their] skill or characteristic rating, a Drall adds a #StarWarsBoost die to the dice pool when providing skilled assistance.
