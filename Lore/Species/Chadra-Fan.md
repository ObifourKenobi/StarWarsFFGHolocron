# Chadra-Fan
## Special Abilities
Chadra-Fan are smaller than average and count as silhouette 0. Chadra-Fan begin the game with one rank in [[Mechanics]]. They still may not train [[Mechanics]] above rank 2 during character creation.
## Additional information
- **Acute Senses:** Chadra-Fan hearing, vision, and olfactory senses are all far superior to most humanoids. They remove up to two #StarWarsSetback dice from their [[Perception]] checks.
