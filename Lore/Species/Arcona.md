# Arcona
The Arcona are slim-framed reptilian humanoids with clawed hands and feet.
## Special Abilities
Arcona begin the game with one rank in [[Vigilance]]. They still may not train [[Vigilance]] above rank 2 during character creation. When making skill checks, Arcona may remove a #StarWarsSetback die imposed due to arid or hot environmental conditions.
## Additional information
- **Mood Readers:** Arcona add an #StarWarsAdvantage result to any [[Charm]] or [[Negotiation]] checks they make.
-  **Salt Addiction:** In general, Arcona count restraint among their virtues, but sodium chloride brings out the worst in them. Often referred to simply as "salt," this compound is the one weakness of an otherwise strong-willed species. The biology of the Arcona predisposes them to crave it. Salt consumption causes vibrant hallucinations as well as irreparable damage to the addict's body, and is ultimately fatal. Salt only affects an Arcona if the salt is relatively pure, so an Arcona can consume salted foods by mistake and not risk any ill effects. If they consume at least two to three grams of pure salt, they are disoriented for the remainder of the encounter. They must also make a #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty #StarWarsDifficulty  [Discipline check](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Discipline_\(Will\) "Discipline (Will)")**. If they fail, they gain 2 Addiction Obligation, if they did not already have an Addiction Obligation. An addicted Arcona's eyes change to a reflective, golden hue. If the Arcona loses **their** Addiction Obligation, **their** eyes return to their natural green.
