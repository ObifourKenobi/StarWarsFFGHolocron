# Bothan
Masters of manipulation and collectors of data, Bothans are known to excel both as spies and as diplomats.
## Special Abilities
Bothans begin the game with one rank in [Streetwise](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Streetwise_\(Cun\) "Streetwise (Cun)"). They still may not train [Streetwise](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Streetwise_\(Cun\) "Streetwise (Cun)") above rank 2 during character creation. They also start with one rank in the [Convincing Demeanor talent](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Convincing_Demeanor_talent "Convincing Demeanor talent").
