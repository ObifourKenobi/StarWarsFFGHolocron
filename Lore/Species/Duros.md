# Duros
## Special Abilities
Duros begin the game with one rank in [[[Piloting-Space]]](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Piloting_-_Space_\(Ag\) "Piloting - Space (Ag)"). They still may not train [[[Piloting-Space]]](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Piloting_-_Space_\(Ag\) "Piloting - Space (Ag)") above rank 2 during character creation.
## Additional information
- **Intuitive Navigation:** Duros may add an [advantage](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Narrative_Dice#Advantage "Narrative Dice") result to all [Astrogation](https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Astrogation_\(Int\) "Astrogation (Int)") checks they make.
