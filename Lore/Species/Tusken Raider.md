# Tusken Raider
## Special Abilities
Tusken Raiders begin the game with one rank in [[Survival]]. They still may not train [[Survival]] above rank 2 during character creation.
## Additionnal information
- **Bantha Riders:** When making [[Athletics]] or [[Survival]] checks to handle a bantha, Tusken Raiders add an automatic #StarWarsSuccess result to the check.
- **Water Reclamator:** Tusken Raiders reduce the #StarWarsDifficulty of all [[Resilience]] and [[Survival]] checks related to enduring extreme heat or going without water once.
