# Model 38 Sharpshooter's Rifle

One of the best-selling firearms in Czerka's Legacy Arms slugthrower product line, each Model 38 is laser measured to accomodate a shooter's natural stance and includes a custom-fitted stock, trigger, and optic for optimal shooting comfort. If purchased used, a Czerka Model 38 loses the Accurate 2 quality until the rifle can be modified to fit the new shooter (requiring a Hard difficulty Mechanics check). CZERKA DETONATOR ROUND While the standard penetrator ammunition offered with the Model 38 works well in most situations, sometimes a shooter needs a little extra punch to fell [their] prey. To this end, Czerka offers the Detonator Round for use with the Model 38.  When used with the Model 38, these rounds have the following effect on the weapon's stats: Remove the Pierce quality and add the Blast (5) quality and Knockdown quality. Detonator Rounds cost 450 credits for a box of rounds, and have a rarity of 7. If the weapon ever runs out of ammo, a new box of rounds must be purchased to reload it.
