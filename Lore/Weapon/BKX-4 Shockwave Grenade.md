# BKX-4 Shockwave Grenade

The BKX-4 is one of a number of grenades built by Baktoid Personal Ordnance, a subsidiary of Baktoid Armor Workshop. BKX-4 SHOCKWAVE GRENADE If the user activates this grenade's Disorient or Knockdown qualities, these qualities affect all characters at short range from the target (even if they are not damaged by the weapon).
