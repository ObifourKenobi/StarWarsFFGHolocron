# ST-M40 Heavy Repeating Blaster Pistol

The ST-M40 is a repeating blaster pistol based loosely on Merr-Sonn's best-selling DD6. ST-M40 HEAVY REPEATING BLASTER PISTOL During combat, GMs may make this weapon run out of ammunition by spending two threat results. This is in addition to the standard spending of a despair result.
