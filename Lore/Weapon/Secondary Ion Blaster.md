# Secondary Ion Blaster

Among the many weapons mounted on the underside of rifles is one that saw a lot of use during the Clone Wars.  SECONDARY ION BLASTER This attachment can be used with any Ranged (Heavy) weapon. Models Include: SoroSuub Droid Defender, BlasTech Model 9 Under-Barrel Ion Blaster. Base Modifiers: Enables the weapon to fire using the following profile at the user's choice: (Ranged [Heavy]; Damage 10; Critical 5; Range [Short]; Disorient 5, Ion). Adds Cumbersome (+1) to weapon and increases encumbrance by 1. Modification Options: 2 Increase ion blaster's damage by +1 Mods.
