# Philaxian Phase-Knife

Developed by an obscure cadre of artificers, these blades were once popular on Philaxia before its discovery by the Republic.  When used with the Saber Throw talent, a character may spend two advantage results or a triumph result (instead of a force point) to have the phase-knife return to their hand. A user may not add lightsaber crystal attachments to a phase-knife. Anyone searching a character carrying a deactivated phase-knife adds a setback die to the Perception check to find the weapon.
