# 12 Defender

Described as a micro blaster in advertising literature, the 12 Defender holdout blaster by Gee-Tech is a weapon of dubious quality and legality. 12 DEFENDER Add two setback dice to a character's Computers and Perception checks when attempting to find a 12 Defender on a person's body. The A 12 Defender may not be reloaded.
