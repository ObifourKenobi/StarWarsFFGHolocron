# FS19 Compound Bow

Traditional, projectile-throwing bow weapons are a rarity in the galaxy. FIELD SPORTS FS19 COMPOUND BOW (BROADHEAD ARROW) The basic ammunition used with the FS19 is Field Sports' Mk. IV Broadhead arrow, which consists of a meter-long plasteel shaft with a sharp, bladed point at one end and synthetic, spin-stabilizing fletching on the other. These arrows are cheap to replace; the GM who wishes to set a price could charge 1 credit per arrow. In addition, Field Sports produces a line of other arrows for use with the FS19 that increase the weapon's versatility.  **Below are** the profiles for non-standard arrows for the bow.
