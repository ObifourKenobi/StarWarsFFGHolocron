# Garrote

Deceptively lethal, garrotes are lengths of line, cord, cable, or chain, often with handles at each end.  GARROTE  A character who makes a successful Brawl attack when using a garrote against a target can cause the target to become suffocated. Targets may attempt to break free of the garrote with an opposed Brawl check, adding a setback die for each round they have been caught.
