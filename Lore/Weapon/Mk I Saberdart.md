# Mk I Saberdart

Kaminoan Weaponsmiths, a subsidiary of Kaminoan Armorsmiths Lts. , is a small weapons producer based on the water planet of Kamino. KWS MK I SABERDART Saberdarts may be loaded with 1 dose of synthetic neuroparalytic, or any other poison the GM deems appropriate. Whenever the wielder successfully attacks a target with the weapon (even if **they** deal no damage), the target also suffers the effects of being exposed to the poison.
