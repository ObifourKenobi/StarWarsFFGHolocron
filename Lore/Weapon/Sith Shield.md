# Sith Shield

The shields used by Sith warriors in ages past can still be found, though they are rare in the extreme. SITH SHIELD Whenever a Force-sensitive wielder of a Sith shield suffers a hit in combat, **they** may commit a Force die to channel **their** fury into the shield as an out of turn incidental. Over multiple attacks, **they** may commit multiple Force dice this way, up to a total number of Force dice no greater than **their** Force rating. As an action, the wielder may uncommit all Force dice committed to the Sith shield to unleash the stored power in a torrent of hate. When **they** do so, **they** gain Conflict equal to the number of Force dice **they** had committed to the shield and then makes a Force power check with that many Force dice. **they** may spend a Force point to inflict 1 wound and 1 strain (these ignore soak) on each other character within short range of **them**; **they** may activate this multiple times, inflicting 1 additional wound and 1 additional strain each time.
