# Welding Rod

Technicians need not only the ability to cut metal apart quickly, but the capacity to fuse it back together. More information about the gear available on the Wookieepedia article. WELDING ROD A welding rod adds an automatic success result to Mechanics checks to repair droids, vehicles, and starships. The GM may spend a despair result on any check made with a welding rod to inflict a Critical Injury on the user.
