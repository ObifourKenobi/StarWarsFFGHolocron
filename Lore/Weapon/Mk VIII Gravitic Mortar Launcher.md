# Mk VIII Gravitic Mortar Launcher

Built by Golan Arms, the Mk VIII launcher is an uncommon weapon. MK VIII GRAVITIC MORTAR LAUNCHER When a gravitic round detonates inside a minefield, it triggers every mine within medium range. Game Masters may use two threat results or a despair result to detonate only a fraction of mines within the gravitic shell's area of effect. Undetonated mines remain active and still pose a threat to Player Characters, their allies, and their vehicles.
