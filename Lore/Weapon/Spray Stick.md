# Spray Stick

An ancient design of the Stokhli people from the Inner Rim world of Manress, the spray stick is a meter-long rod shouldered like a rifle that fires a concentrated spraynet mist. When using the stun pad on the butt-stock, the Stokhli Spray Stick can be used as a melee weapon with the following profile (Skill Melee; Damage +2; Critical ; Range [Engaged]; Inaccurate 1, Stun Damage)SPRAY STICK (MELEE PROFILE)
