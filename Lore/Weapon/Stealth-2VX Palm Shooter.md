# Stealth-2VX Palm Shooter

Considered by many to be the smallest ranged weapon in the galaxy, the Prax Arms Stealth-2VX palm shooter can, as then name suggests, easily fit in the palm of a user's hand. Each dart can be loaded with one dose of neurotoxin, anesthetic, neuroparalytic, or any other poison the GM deems appropriate. Whenever the wielder successfully attack a target with the weapon (whether or not they deal damage), the target also suffers the effects of being exposed to the poison. Add two setback dice to Perception checks to find a Stealth-2VX Palm Shooter on a wearer's body.
