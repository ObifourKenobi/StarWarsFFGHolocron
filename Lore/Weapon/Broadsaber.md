# Broadsaber

Through specialized focusing lenses and emitters, this lightsaber hilt produces a blade that is broader and flatter than a standard lightsaber's cylindrical blade, lending it a profile closer to that of a traditional metal sword. BROADSABER This weapon's profile represents a broadsaber hilt containing an unmodded Ilum crystal. This crystal occupies two of the weapon's hard points. When installing a lightsaber crystal into a broadsaber, increase the damage of that lightsaber crystal by 1.
