# J-10 Dual Blaster Cannon

Originally a Separatist design from the Clone Wars, the J-10 is essentially a scaled-up pair of reciprocating E-5 blaster rifles. The GM can spend two threat results or a despair result from any combat check made with this weapon to have it overheat. The wielder suffers 2 strain and the weapon becomes damaged one step.
