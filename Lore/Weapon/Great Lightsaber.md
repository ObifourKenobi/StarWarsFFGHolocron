# Great Lightsaber

Great lightsabers are oversized lightsabers built for individuals with large, powerful frames.  This lightsaber is a great lightsaber hilt containing an unmodded Ilum lightsaber crystal. This crystal occupies two of the weapon's hard points. When installing a lightsaber crystal into a great lightsaber increase the damage of that lightsaber crystal by one.
