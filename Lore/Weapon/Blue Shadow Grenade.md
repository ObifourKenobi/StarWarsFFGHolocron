# Blue Shadow Grenade

Of all viruses, epidemiologists most fear one strain: the waterborne blue shadow virus. When a Blue Shadow grenade detonates, all targets who suffer at least 1 wound from the grenade—or are within medium range of the detonation while not wearing a rebreather, sealed armor, or other form of environmental protection—must make a Formidable difficulty Resilience check. Targets suffer 2 wounds and 2 strain per threat result, and a Critical Injury with a +50 modified if they fail the check. A despair result means they must make the check again at the start of their next turn as the virus runs rampant through their system.
