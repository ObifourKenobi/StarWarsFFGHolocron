# CryoBan Rifle

Loronar's CryoBan rifle is in the tradition of Ulban Arms and several other manufacturers who have weaponized CryoBan technology. When used to attack a target wearing armor that removes one or more setback dice due to extreme cold, the weapon's Pierce quality counts as 0. Characters who make a melee attack against a target struck by a CryoBan rifle in the current round add two boost dice to the check.
