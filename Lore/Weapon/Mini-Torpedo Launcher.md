# Mini-Torpedo Launcher

The Mon Cal Defenses mini-torpedo launcher is a portable weapon designed exclusively for sub-aquatic combat situations. MON CAL DEFENSES MINI-TORPEDO LAUNCHER The mini-torpedo launcher can only be used effectively underwater. All combat checks to operate it outside of water have their difficulty upgraded twice. The profile provided with this weapon is for the default torpedoes. Specialized torpedoes can be loaded into the launcher. While these torpedoes are loaded into the launcher. While these torpedoes are loaded ,they replace the damage, critical rating, range, and item qualities of the original weapon with their own. Regardless of what is loaded in the launcher, it maintains the Cumbersome quality.
