# Rivet Gun

Most rivet guns work around the principle of a basic pneumatic hammer, and fire bolts into vehicle plating. More information about the gear available on the Wookieepedia article. RIVET GUN A rivet gun reduces the difficulty of Mechanics checks to repair Critical Hits on vehicles and starships by 1 (to a minimum of Simple [-]). CUSTOM ATTACHMENT Top-Loading Magazine: By switching out the standard single-bolt loading system for a cobbled-together magazine containing numerous bolts, a skilled craftsperson can increase the number of rivets the gun can place per minute dramatically. Base Modifiers: Removes the Limited Ammo 1 quality. Modification Options: None. Hard Points Required: 1. Price/Rarity: 50/1. Encumbrance: 0. CUSTOM ATTACHMENT Balanced Barrel: Adding a functional barrel to a rivet gun can give it dramatically more range and accuracy as a shooting weapon. Base Modifiers: Increases range to short. Modification Options: 2 decrease Inaccurate quality by 1 Mods. Hard Points Required: 1. Price/Rarity: 150/3. Encumbrance: 1.
