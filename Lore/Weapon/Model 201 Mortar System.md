# Model 201 Mortar System

The Model 201 mortar is a portable light artillery system used by specialized clone artillery troopers. MODEL 201 MORTAR SYSTEM The weapon consists of three parts—the launcher, a heavy bipod, and the ammunition magazine—and is operated by a specially trained mortar team. A mortar team consists of three clones: one to carry the weapon, one to carry the bipod, and one to carry the ammo. The Model 201 can only be used at medium range or further due to the arc of its shells.
