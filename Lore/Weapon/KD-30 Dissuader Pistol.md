# KD-30 Dissuader Pistol

A particularly vicious slugthrower, the KD-30 Dissuader fires special ammunition filled with a particularly caustic acid capable of eating through armor, flesh, and bone. KELVAREK CONSOLIDATED ARMS KD-30 DISSUADER PISTOL The acid in the Dissuader's ammunition continues to cause damage over time. A target struck by a round from the KD-30 suffers one wound, ignoring soak, at the beginning of [their] turn for the next two rounds.
