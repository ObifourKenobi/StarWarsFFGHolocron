# Chewbacca's Bowcaster

Handcrafted shortly before Chewbacca's hrrtayyk coming-of-age ceremony, Chewie's bowcaster has been helping him fulfill his life debt to Han Solo for years. CHEWBACCA'S BOWCASTER Quarrel Bandolier: Once per encounter, the wielder may spend a maneuver to load a specialized quarrel and either add the Blast 6, Breach 1, Concussive 1, Disorient 2, Ion, or Vicious 4 quality to the next combat check made with Chewbacca's Bowcaster. PROTECTIVE FIRE As an action, Chewbacca may make a Hard difficulty Coercion or Ranged (Heavy) check. If he succeeds, one rival or minion group within medium range is staggered until the end of their next turn. Chewie may spend two advantage results to affect an additional rival or minion group within range and may spend a triumph result to force affected targets to spend their next maneuver to move away from Chewbacca.
