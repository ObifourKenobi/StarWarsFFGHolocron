# Whipcord Thrower

A whipcord thrower quickly deploys a length of high-tensile-strength cable, firing it over a distance in order to secure a target.   More information about the attachment available on the Wookieepedia article. WHIPCORD THROWER  Models Include: Jeron Hookless Grapplers, SoroSuub Whipcord Thrower. Base Modifiers: Whipcord throwers can be used as climbing gear to assist the wearer in climbing a steep vertical surface, reducing the difficulty to climb by one. They can support a weight of up to 200 kilograms. Further, the thrower may be used as a weapon with the following profile: (Melee; Damage +1; Critical 5; Range [Short]; Ensnare 2). Modification Options: None.
