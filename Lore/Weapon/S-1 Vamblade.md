# S-1 Vamblade

Falling somewhere between brass knuckles and a sword, vamblades are dagger-length blades extending from durasteel vambraces, which mount the blade and provide a degree of protection to the wielder's forearms.  S-1 VAMBLADE  Vamblades are designed to be worn as a pair and open up a whole range of combat options when wielded by a properly trained fighter. When used as a pair, these wristblades gain the Sunder and Accurate 1 item qualities. Vamblades cannot deal damage to a target's strain threshold.
