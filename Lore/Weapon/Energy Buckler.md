# Energy Buckler

An energy buckler consists of a metallic disk worn on the wielder's wrist. ENERGY BUCKLER When the wielder is using an energy buckler, the GM can spend any despair result the wielder generates on a Combat check to have the energy buckler run out of power; it ceases to function until the end of the encounter or until recharged.
