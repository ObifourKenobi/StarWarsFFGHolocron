# Electrostaff

Electrostaffs first saw widespread use during the Clone Wars among Separatist forces, especially among the personal guard droids of General Grievous.  Models Include: Baktoid Armor Workshop vz. 890 Thunderer, Arakyd Industries Stryker. ELECTROSTAFF Electrostaves require two hands to use.
