# MMD-18 Molecular Dagger

The MMD-18 is a larger, more durable version of Xana Exotic Arms' MSW-9 molecular stiletto.  More information about the weapon available on the Wookieepedia article.MMD-18 MOLECULAR DAGGER[1][2][]Due to the MMD-18's inherent fragility, any combat checks made that generate either three threat results or a despair result while wielding one shatters the blade, destroying the weapon.
