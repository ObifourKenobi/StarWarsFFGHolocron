# Boonta Blaster

A Huttese weapon of sinister design, the Boonta Blaster is named for the infamous, deified Hutt who allegedly commissioned the first one: Boonta Hestilic Shad'ruu. Characters add one setback die to Perception checks to realize a scepter is a Boonta Blaster.   Add a setback die to checks to find a Boonta Blaster on a wearer's body.  Remove one setback die from the wearer's Deception checks to explain away the weapon as purely ornamental or otherwise nonthreatening.
