# Heavy Blaster Pistol

Nearly as powerful as most blaster rifles, heavy blaster pistols are large, bulky sidearms that are nearly impossible to conceal. Game Masters have the option to spend three threat results or a despair result to force a heavy blaster pistol to run out of ammo.
