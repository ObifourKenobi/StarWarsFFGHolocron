# Heavy Blaster Pistol

Nearly as powerful as most blaster rifles, heavy blaster pistols are large, bulky sidearms that are nearly impossible to conceal.More information about the weapon available on the Wookieepedia article.Models Include: BlasTech DT-57 Annihilator, BlasTech DL-44, SoroSuub SSK-7, Merr-Sonn Model 434.HEAVY BLASTER PISTOL[1][2][3][]Game Masters have the option to spend three threat results or a despair result to force a heavy blaster pistol to run out of ammo.[1][4][5]
