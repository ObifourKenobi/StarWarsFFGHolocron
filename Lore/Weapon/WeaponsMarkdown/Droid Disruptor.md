# Droid Disruptor

Verpine weaponsmiths enjoy creating specialized weapons, such as the Verpine droid disruptor. More information about the weapon available on the Wookieepedia article.DROID DISRUPTOR[1][]When used against organic creatures, the droid disruptor is much less effective. Any organic creature stuck by a droid disruptor does not receive normal damage, but instead simply experiences a brief shock to the system and suffers 2 strain.[2]
