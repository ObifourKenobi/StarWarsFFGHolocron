# Mk.4 Heavy Fragmentation Grenade

Sporting a stronger detonite charge and larger surface area than other frag grenades, Golan Arms' heavy fragmentation grenade yields a consequently larger and more powerful blast, with a greater quantity of shrapnel dispersed over a wider area.
