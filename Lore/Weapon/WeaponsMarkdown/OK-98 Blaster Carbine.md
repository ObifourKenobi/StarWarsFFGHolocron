# OK-98 Blaster Carbine

A unique SoroSuub design featuring twin power packs to double ammunition capacity, the OK-98 sold poorly even though it performed adequately. More information about the weapon available on the Wookieepedia article.OK-98 BLASTER CARBINE[1][2][]A character using an OK-98 can ignore the first out of ammo despair result during a combat, just as if they had an extra reload, without expending a maneuver. Modifying an OK-98 (requiring an Easy difficulty Mechanics check) increases its damage by two, replacing the option to ignore the first out of ammo result. The GM can spend a despair result to have a modified OK-98 catastrophically overheat and explode, inflicting one hit from the weapon (dealing base damage) on the user and destroying the weapon after the attack has been resolved.[1][3]
