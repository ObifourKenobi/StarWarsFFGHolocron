# ELG-3A Blaster Pistol

A crossover weapon that occupied a unique niche between holdout blaster and light blaster pistol, the elegant ELG-3A is a SoroSuub Corporation design marketed to politicians and the wealthy, who demand both form and function. Add two setback dice to a character's Perception checks to find an ELG-3A on a person's body.
