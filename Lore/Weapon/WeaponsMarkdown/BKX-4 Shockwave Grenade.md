# BKX-4 Shockwave Grenade

The BKX-4 is one of a number of grenades built by Baktoid Personal Ordnance, a subsidiary of Baktoid Armor Workshop. More information about the weapon available on the Wookieepedia article.BKX-4 SHOCKWAVE GRENADE[1][]If the user activates this grenade's Disorient or Knockdown qualities, these qualities affect all characters at short range from the target (even if they are not damaged by the weapon).[2]
