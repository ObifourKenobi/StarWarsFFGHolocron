# Gungan Electropole

Gungan herders traditionally use electropoles to guide and guard their shaaks.  More information about the weapon available on the Wookieepedia article.GUNGAN ELECTROPOLE[1][]An electropole may be thrown as a ranged weapon. If used in this manner, change the skill to Ranged (Light) and the range of the weapon to short. The weapon also gains the Limited Ammo 1 quality (but can be retrieved and used again).[2]
