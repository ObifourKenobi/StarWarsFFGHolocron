# Vac Attack Mk-127 Concealable Disc Launcher

Initially designed by the VacuTech Combine—which ran out of capital and declared bankruptcy during the development of this weapon thanks to Imperial meddling—the Vac Attack is a small projectile launcher worn around the wrist like a chrono.More information about the weapon available on the Wookieepedia article.VAC ATTACK MK-127 CONCEALABLE DISK LAUNCHER[1][]Changing the weapon's spread from single to arc requires a maneuver and grants the weapon the Blast 6 item quality. If Blast is activated, the weapon's supply of discs is exhausted in the shot. When worn on both wrists, Mk-127s can be fired as if they were a single weapon with a regular combat check, gaining the Linked 2 quality. Noticing that an individual is wearing one or a pair of these weapons requires a Hard difficulty Perception check. At the Game Master's discretion the discs can be coated with various toxins. Reloads for the Mk-127 contain four discs and cost 100 credits.[2]
