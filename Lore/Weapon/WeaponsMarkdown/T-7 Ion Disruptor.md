# T-7 Ion Disruptor

A large ion disruptor rifle built for taking out aircraft and armored vehicles, the T-7 ion disruptor is a notorious weapon with a shameful past. Like other disruptor weapons, T-7 ion disruptors may never benefit from upgrades or attachments that improve their range or grant multiple shots.[3]Whenever a living target is hit with a T-7 ion disruptor, any Critical Injury results in a Crippled effect, unless the [Critical Injury roll] would result in a more grievous injury.[3]Whenever a vehicle or starship is hit with a T-7 ion disruptor, any Critical Hit results in a Component Hit effect unless the Critical Hit roll results in a more serious effect.
