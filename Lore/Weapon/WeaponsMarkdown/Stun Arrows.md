# Stun Arrows

A massive bow made of composite materials, the compound bow uses a system of cams and cables to lever the arms and reduce the draw weight when fully drawn, allowing for longer, more accurate aiming.  CORELLIAN COMPOUND BOW Regular arrows are cheap to replace; if the GM needs to set a price [they] can charge 1 credit per arrow.
