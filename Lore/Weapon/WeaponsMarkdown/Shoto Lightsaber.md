# Shoto Lightsaber

A miniaturized lightsaber, the shoto was initially designed as an offhand weapon for duelists training in two-handed Jar'Kai lightsaber combat.  Models Include: None; each shoto is individually crafted.  DURING THE CLONE WARS: SHOTO[3][]This lightsaber is a basic shoto hilt containing an unmodded Ilum lightsaber crystal. This crystal occupies two of the weapon's hard points.[1][3][2]When installing a lightsaber crystal into a shoto, reduce the damage of that lightsaber crystal by one.[1][3][2]Lightsabers cannot be Sundered.[4][5]
