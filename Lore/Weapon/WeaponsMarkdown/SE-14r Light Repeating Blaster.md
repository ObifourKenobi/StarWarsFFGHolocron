# SE-14r Light Repeating Blaster

The BlasTech SE-14r, also known as the Imperial repeating pistol, combines the portability and ease of a blaster pistol with a high rate of fire.More information about the weapon available on the Wookieepedia article.BLASTECH SE-14R LIGHT REPEATING BLASTER[1][]Due to its high rate of fire, the SE-14r is more likely to run out of ammo during an extended firefight or series of engagements. The GM may spend three threat results to cause a character's SE-14r to run out of ammo.[2]
