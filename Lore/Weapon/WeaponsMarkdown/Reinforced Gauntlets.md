# Reinforced Gauntlets

Additional armor plating on the hands is a valuable upgrade for those who favor heavier armor, as well as for those who do welding jobs on starships and desire to keep all of their fingers.  REINFORCED GAUNTLETS This attachment can be applied to any armor with soak 2 or higher. Models Include: Various models. Base Modifiers: The character can use the gauntlets as a weapon with the following profile: (Brawl; Damage +1; Critical 4; Range [Engaged]; Disorient 3). Modification Options: 1 Innate Talent (Durable) Mod.
