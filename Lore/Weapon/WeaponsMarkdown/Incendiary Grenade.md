# Incendiary Grenade

Highly restricted and banned on most civilized worlds, incendiary grenades are anti-personnel grenades that cause damage with fire and heat rather than with an explosive charge.  Models Include: Wynz-Tek Mk IV Firestorm, Merr-Sonn D-24 Inferno Grenade. More information about the weapon available on the Wookieepedia article.INCENDIARY GRENADE[1][]
