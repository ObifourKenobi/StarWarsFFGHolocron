# Shock Boots

A specialty item crafted for those determined not to be caught defenseless under any circumstances, shock boots use the same technology as shock gloves.  Shock boots are worn on the feet, and thus do not require a hand to wield. Add a setback die to a character's Perception checks to notice that shock boots are a weapon rather than normal footwear.[1][3]
