# Wipe-3 Data-Purge Grenade

SoroSuub's Wipe-3 data-purge grenade erases electronically stored data from all devices within its blast radius with a brief, but intense, electromagnetic pulse. More information about the weapon available on the Wookieepedia article.WIPE-3 DATA-PURGE GRENADE[1][2][]Any computer or similar device hit by a data-purge grenade loses all programming, memory, and any other kind of electronically stored data but remains otherwise undamaged. A droid hit by a data-purge grenade must succeed on a Hard difficulty Resilience check or suffer 1  strain, plus 1 additional strain per failure result. At the GM's discretion, three threat results or a despair result on the check can cause the droid to lose portions of its memory. TRAPS AND TRIPWIRES[2][]As with frag grenades, a character can set [...] data-purge grenades [...] to detonate on impact, set them with a timer to detonate up to three rounds after being activated, or set up the explosive with connected a pressure detonator.
