# Nightstinger Blaster Rifle

Officially classified as a sporting blaster, Xerrol Corporation's Nightstinger was marketed as a powerful, well-balanced rifle for big game hunting, which, while true, omits its ability to fire completely invisible blaster bolts with the flick of a switch. More information about the weapon available on the Wookieepedia article.NIGHTSTINGER BLASTER RIFLE[1][2][]Switching the Nightstinger to stealth mode requires a maneuver. Stealth mode makes locating the shooter by tracing the blaster bolt or spotting a muzzle blast effectively impossible. It also grants the weapon the Slow-Firing 2 item quality for the remainder for the encounter.[1][3]
