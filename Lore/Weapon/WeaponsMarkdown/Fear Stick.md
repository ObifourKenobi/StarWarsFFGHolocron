# Fear Stick

The fear stick is only the size of a stylus or a hypodermic injector and injects a potent neurologic compound in a target's system.  Add two setback dice to Perception checks to find a fear stick on a wearer's body. On a successful hit with a fear stick, the attacker may spend a triumph result to force a living target to make a Formidable difficulty fear check. Fear sticks have no effect on droids or nonliving creatures.[1][3]
