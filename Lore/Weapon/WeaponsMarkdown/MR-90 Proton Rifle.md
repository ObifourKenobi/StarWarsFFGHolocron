# MR-90 Proton Rifle

One of the more powerful rifles in the Merr-Sonn Munitions inventory, the MR-90 proton rifle is a particle beam weapon originally designed for hunting the biggest, most dangerous prey in the galaxy. More information about the weapon available on the Wookieepedia article.MR-90 PROTON RIFLE[1][]OR[2][]As a maneuver, a character using an MR-90 can set the weapon to its charge setting, which allows the shooter to expend all of the rifle's energy in a single, devastating blast. Once charged, the MR-90 counts as having the Breach 1 and Concussive 2 item qualities. After the charged beam is fired, the weapon runs out of ammunition.[1][3]
