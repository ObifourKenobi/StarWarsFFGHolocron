# Findsman Shockprod

Findsmen shockprods are meter-long metal-alloy polearms with a forked end tipped with stun electrodes.  More information about the weapon available on the Wookieepedia article.FINDSMAN SHOCKPROD[1][]When a character wielding a Findsman shockprod makes a Seek, Sense, or Farsight Force power check, they may add one force point to the results.This weapon requires 2 hands to wield.
