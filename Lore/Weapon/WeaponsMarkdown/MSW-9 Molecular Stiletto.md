# MSW-9 Molecular Stiletto

Produced by Xana Exotic Arms, the MSW-9 is a small, easily concealable blade that is popular among professionals, politicians, and entertainers.  More information about the weapon available on the Wookieepedia article.MSW-9 MOLECULAR STILETTO[1][2][]Due to the blade's inherent fragility, any combat check made that generates either two threat results or a despair result while wielding one damages the weapon by one step (as if struck by a weapon with the Sunder quality[3]). Add a setback die to a character's Perception check when attempting to find a molecular stiletto on a person's body.[1][3]
