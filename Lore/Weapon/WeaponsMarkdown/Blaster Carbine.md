# Blaster Carbine

Blaster carbines are small-framed version of common blaster rifles, designed to deliver the power of a rifle in a smaller, easier-to-use package.More information about the weapon available on the Wookieepedia article.Models Include: BlasTech EE-3, BlasTech DC-15S, SoroSuub E-11 Carbine, Baktoid Armor Workshop E5 Carbine.BLASTER CARBINE[1][2][3][]Blaster carbines may be fired one-handed with no penalties at short range.[1]
