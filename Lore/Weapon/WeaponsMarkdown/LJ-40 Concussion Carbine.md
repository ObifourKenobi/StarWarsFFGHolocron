# LJ-40 Concussion Carbine

The LJ-40 has its origins in the Clone Wars, when heavy infantry squads received early shipments of Arakyd Industries LJ-50 concussion rifles. More information about the weapon available on the Wookieepedia article.LJ-40 CONCUSSION CARBINE[1][2][]All setback dice added to a combat check due to the target's cover are removed when using the LJ-40. Additional ammunition costs 40 credits for a magazine of three rounds.
