# Panic Pistol

The Panic Pistol is Oriolanis Defense Systems' compact civilian self-defense slugthrower pistol. More information about the weapon available on the Wookieepedia article.PANIC PISTOL[1][2][]While at engaged range, a character wielding a Panic Pistol does not add the +1 difficulty modifier for ranged attacks in melee. However, the GM may spend two threat results or a despair result to damage the weapon one step.[1][3]
