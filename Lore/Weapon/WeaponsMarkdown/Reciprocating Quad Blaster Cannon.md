# Reciprocating Quad Blaster Cannon

What is essentially a downscaled version of an anti-starfighter emplacement, the reciprocating quad blaster cannon is the heaviest personal weapon in Merr-Sonn Munitions' entire arsenal. RECIPROCATING QUAD BLASTER CANNON This weapon can only be used in Auto-fire mode. The GM may spend a despair result or four threat results from a combat check with this weapon to have the wielder suffer a Critical Injury with -50 to the roll, to a minimum of one.
