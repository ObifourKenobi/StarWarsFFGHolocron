# MWC-35c Staccato Lightning Repeating Cannon

Morellian Weapons Conglomerate MWC-35c heavy repeating blaster cannon is one of the galaxy's premier support weapons. MWC-35C STACCATOR LIGHTNING REPEATING CANNON (STANDARD MODE) (SINGLE-SHOT MODE) Switching between modes requires a maneuver. If this weapon is ever destroyed—by a weapon with the Sunder quality or through other means determined by the Game Master—it blows up and deals 10 damage to the wearer and other characters within engaged range.
