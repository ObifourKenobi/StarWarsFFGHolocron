# DE-10 Blaster Pistol

The DE-10 is a stylish and reliable heavy blaster produced by BlasTech subsidiary Antrech Arms, specialists in boutique and niche products. DE-10 BLASTER PISTOL The DE-10's telescopic optical sight reduces the difficulty of combat checks made at long and extreme range by one. A DE-10 in need of minor repairs does not add a setback die to checks. Add a boost die to checks made to repair a damaged DE-10.
