# NX-14 Needler

Needlers refer to an obscure series of dart shooters that rely on electromagnetic rail propulsion. A needler removes all setback dice added by a personal deflector shield. Anyone searching an individual carrying a concealed needler adds one setback die to their Perception check to find the weapon.
