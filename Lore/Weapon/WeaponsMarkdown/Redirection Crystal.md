# Redirection Crystal

Redirection crystals are an invention of the Imperial Security Bureau. REDIRECTION CRYSTAL Setting up a redirection crystal requires a Hard difficulty Skulduggery check. Due to its size, hitting a redirection crystal requires a Hard difficulty Ranged (Light) or Ranged (Heavy) check. Redirection crystals have the following profile: (Damage 9; Critical 4; Range [Short]; Limited Ammo 1). They cost 500 credits (R) and have a rarity of 9.
