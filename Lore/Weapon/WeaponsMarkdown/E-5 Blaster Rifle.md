# E-5 Blaster Rifle

The E-5 blaster rifle is a copy of a BlasTech design produced by Baktoid Armor Workshop for the Separatist armed forces. E-5 BLASTER RIFLE If an E-5 suffers damage, it suffers one additional step of damage. Game Masters may spend a despair result or three threat results generated on a combat check with an E-5 to cause the weapon to overheat. This damages the weapon and deals 2 wounds to the wielder if they are organic.
