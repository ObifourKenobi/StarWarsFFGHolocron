# Z-6 Rotary Blaster Cannon

The Z-6 rotary blaster cannon is an exceptionally potent heavy squad support weapon built for the GAR by BlasTech.More information about the weapon available on the Wookieepedia article.Z-6 ROTARY BLASTER CANNON[1][2][]This weapon can only be used with its Auto-fire item quality active. The GM may spend a despair result or two threat results from any combat check made with this weapon to have it overheat. When the weapon overheats, the wielder suffers 2 strain, and the weapon becomes damaged one step, from undamaged to minor, minor to moderate, or moderate to major.[1][3]
