# Missile Tube

Missile tubes are shoulder-fired, portable launchers capable of firing a variety of small but deadly warheads.  Additional ammo for a missile tube costs 500 credits per rack of six.[1][5] Other missiles may be substituted for the standard type, replacing the damage, critical rating, range, and special qualities with that of the alternate missile. Alternate missiles may be purchased for their listed price.[1]
