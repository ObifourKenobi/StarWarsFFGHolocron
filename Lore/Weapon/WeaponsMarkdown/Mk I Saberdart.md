# Mk I Saberdart

Kaminoan Weaponsmiths, a subsidiary of Kaminoan Armorsmiths Lts., is a small weapons producer based on the water planet of Kamino.More information about the weapon available on the Wookieepedia article.KWS MK I SABERDART[1][]Saberdarts may be loaded with 1 dose of synthetic neuroparalytic, or any other poison the GM deems appropriate. Whenever the wielder successfully attacks a target with the weapon (even if [they] deal no damage), the target also suffers the effects of being exposed to the poison.[2]
