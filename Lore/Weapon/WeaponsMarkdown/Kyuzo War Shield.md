# Kyuzo War Shield

Originally utilitarian in function, the Kyuzo war shield was designed by the inhabitants of the Kwaidan Peaks, as a headpiece to protect travelers from torrential rains during monsoon season in southern Phatrong.  More information about the weapon available on the Wookieepedia article.KYUZO WAR SHIELD[1][]A Kyuzo war shield may be thrown as a ranged weapon. If used in this manner, change the skill to Ranged (Light) and the range of the weapon to short. The weapon also gains the Limited Ammo 1 quality (but if retrieved, it can be used again).Additionally, the wielder of a Kyuzo war shield may spend three advantage results from any combat check to throw the weapon to cause the shield to ricochet back to [their] grasp.
