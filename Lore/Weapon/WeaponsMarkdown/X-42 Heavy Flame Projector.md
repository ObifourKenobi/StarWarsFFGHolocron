# X-42 Heavy Flame Projector

The BT X-42 heavy flame projector, from BlasTech, is one of the most destructive and terrifying weapons in the GAR's arsernal. BT X-42 HEAVY FLAME PROJECTOR Game Masters can spend three threat results to cause the weapon to overheat, leaving it inoperable for three turns, or a despair result to have the weapon explode. An exploding X-42 deals full damage to the user and Blast damage to anything engaged with them.
