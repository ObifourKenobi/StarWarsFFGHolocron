# CS-12 Stun Master

Produced by SoroSuub and popular with many Sullustans, the CS-12 Stun Master is one of the more ubiquitous non-lethal self-defense weapons in the galaxy.  Add a setback die to a character's Perception checks to find a Stun Master on a person's body.
