# Ionization Blaster

Ionization blasters are short-ranged weapons used to disable droids and other electronics.More information about the weapon available on the Wookieepedia article.Models Include: Merr-Sonn DEMP 2 Ion Carbine, scavenged Jawa scrap ion blaster.IONIZATION BLASTER[1][2][]OR[3][][...] Ion blasters have little effect on sentient beings and deal only strain damage to droids, cyborgs, and vehicles as dictated by their Ion quality.[4]Ionization blasters work only on droids and cyborgs as per their Stun Quality. They have no effect on organic beings beyond a brief and painful sting.[5]
