# Ascian Throwing Dagger

Named after the binary star system from where they supposedly originate, Ascian daggers are finely weighted and balanced for throwing.More information about the weapon available on the Wookieepedia article.ASCIAN THROWING DAGGER[1][]When making an attack with an Ascian throwing dagger, a character does not increase the difficulty of the check for being engaged with [their] target as normal. The user adds [their] Brawn to the listed value (+1) to determine the damage dealt with this weapon.[2]
