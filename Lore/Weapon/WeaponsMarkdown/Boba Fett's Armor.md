# Boba Fett's Armor

Clad in sage-green Mandalorian armor with red trim, Boba Fett draws on the history of Mandalore's legendary warriors to strike fear in his targets.  More information about the armor available on the Wookieepedia article.BOBA FETT'S ARMOR[1][]Boba Fett's customized Mandalorian armor comes equipped with an enhanced optics suite (remove two setback dice from Perception, Vigilance, and combat checks due to environmental effects that obscure vision), several weapon systems, an integrated jetpack with rocket, and range finder (adds an automatic advantage result on all integrated weapon attacks).Integrated Armor Weapons: Flamethrower (Ranged [Light]; Damage 5; Critical 2; Range [Short]; Blast 2, Burn 2, Pierce 2, Vicious 3), jetpack missile tube (Gunnery; Damage 20; Critical 2; Range [Extreme]; Blast 10, Breach 1, Cumbersome 3, Guided 3, Limited Ammo 1, Prepare 1), whipcord (Ranged [Light]; Damage 1; Critical –; Range [Short]; Ensnare 3, Knockdown, Limited Ammo 1).Premeditated Contingency: Once per encounter as a maneuver, a character wearing Boba Fett's armor may make an Average difficulty Vigilance check to draw any weapon or item of a rarity equal to or less than the total number of net success and advantage results at the GM's discretion.HEARTLESS SILENCE[]Once per session, as an out-of-turn incidental [Boba Fett] may spend a Destiny Point to cause a social check targeting him to fail. The character may not attempt another social skill check targeting Boba Fett for the remainder of the encounter.
