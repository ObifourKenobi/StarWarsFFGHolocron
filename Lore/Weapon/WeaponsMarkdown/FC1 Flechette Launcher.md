# FC1 Flechette Launcher

Rebel partisans have used the now-banned Golan Arms FC1 flechette launcher in a number of attacks, most notoriously at Inusagi's Sakoola Blossom Festival, killing hundreds of guests and just-announced Imperial Governor Tophervin. FC1 FLECHETTE LAUNCHER (ANTI-VEHICLE) When using anti-vehicle canisters against targets of silhouette 1 or smaller, the weapons gains the Inaccurate 3 quality.[3]Some mercenaries tend to load the FC1 with a mix of both, so that they can respond to any situation. In this case, the character should not what [their] weapon is loaded with, and use the appropriate profile with each shot. [They] still have to track total shots as per the Limited Ammo quality.
