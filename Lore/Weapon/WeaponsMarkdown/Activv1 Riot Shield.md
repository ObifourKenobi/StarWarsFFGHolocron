# Activv1 Riot Shield

Drearian Defense Conglomerate's Activv1 riot shield is one of the most common models in the galaxy, fielded by officers responding to riots and violent protests.  Riot shields can only be used with one-handed weapons, but some incorporate a slot or mag lock to support a Ranged (Heavy) weapon with the shield itself (users fire the weapon one-handed while wielding the riot shield in the other hand). A riot shield with this feature costs an additional 100 credits and has no hard points. Firing a Ranged (Heavy) rifle locked to the shield this way adds a setback die to combat checks with both weapons.
