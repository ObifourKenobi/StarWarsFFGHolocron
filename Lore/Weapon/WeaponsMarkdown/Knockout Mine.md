# Knockout Mine

Another favorite tool for nonlethal personnel control, knockout charges such as Brennkeyes Syndicate safety mines allow the user to safely concentrate on other tasks while being assured early warning in the case of unwanted guests—at least, those who need to breathe. BRENNKEYES SYNDICATE KNOCKOUT MINES[1][]A character hit by a knockout mine must make a Hard difficulty Resilience check. If [they] fail, [they] become immobilized until the end of the encounter.  TRAPS AND TRIPWIRES[1][]As with frag grenades, a character can set [...] knockout mines [...] to detonate on impact, set them with a timer to detonate up to three rounds after being activated, or set up the explosive with connected a pressure detonator.
