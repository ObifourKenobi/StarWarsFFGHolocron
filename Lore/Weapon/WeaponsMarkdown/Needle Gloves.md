# Needle Gloves

To all appearances, needle gloves look like typical hand coverings; however, with a specific hand gesture, the wearer can extend an ultrathin needle that is otherwise nearly undetectable.  More information about the weapon available on the Wookieepedia article.NEEDLE GLOVES[1][2][]Needle gloves may be loaded with one dose of neurotoxin, anesthetic, neuroparalytic, or any other poison the GM deems appropriate. Whenever the wielder successfully attacks a target with the weapon (even if they deal no damage), the target also suffers the effects of being exposed to the poison. Add three setback dice to a character's Perception checks to determine that a set of needle gloves contains a lethal weapon.
