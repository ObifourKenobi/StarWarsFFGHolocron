# Model 201 Mortar System

The Model 201 mortar is a portable light artillery system used by specialized clone artillery troopers.More information about the weapon available on the Wookieepedia article.MODEL 201 MORTAR SYSTEM[1][]The weapon consists of three parts—the launcher, a heavy bipod, and the ammunition magazine—and is operated by a specially trained mortar team. A mortar team consists of three clones: one to carry the weapon, one to carry the bipod, and one to carry the ammo.[2]The Model 201 can only be used at medium range or further due to the arc of its shells.[2]
