# Aurateran Boomerang

Although Auraterans have access to modern energy weapons, the simply but reliable boomerang is still a popular weapon for local hunters and trappers. More information about the weapon available on the Wookieepedia article.AURATERAN BOOMERANG[1][]If retrieved, a thrown boomerang can be used again. A character may spend an advantage result on a missed attack (including an attack made with the Guided quality) to have the boomerang return to the user's hand.
