# HOB Heavy Repeating Blaster

The HOB, or high-output repeating blaster, is the SoroSuub Corporation's attempt to loosen BlasTech's and Merr-Sonn's strangleholds on the heavy repeater market. HOB HEAVY REPEATING BLASTER The price of this weapon includes the price of a tripod and power pack.
