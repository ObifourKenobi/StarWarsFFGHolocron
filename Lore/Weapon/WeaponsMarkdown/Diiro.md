# Diiro

Often carried by Quolas's guards and hunters, the diiro is a common Arbooinian weapon, tailored to the needs of the Sathari.  DIIRO * (Arbooine only)A diiro can be loaded with up to three doses of any poison (most locals use bearsloth venom). Whenever the wielder successfully attacks a target with a diiro (whether or not damage is dealt), the target also suffers the effects of being exposed to the poison it contains. Due to its lightweight construction, when a diiro suffers one or more levels of damage due to a despair result on a combat check or the effects of the Sunder quality, it receives one additional level of damage.
