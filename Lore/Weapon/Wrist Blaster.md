# Wrist Blaster

Wrist blasters are custom-made self-defense weapons worn by diplomats, espionage agents, businesspeople, and other individuals who need an inconspicuous personal defense weapon. WRIST BLASTER Noticing that an individual is wearing a wrist blaster requires a Daunting difficulty Perception check. At the Game Master's discretion, three threat results or a despair result can be spent on a user's Ranged (Light) check to have the weapon burn the user and deal 3 wounds.
