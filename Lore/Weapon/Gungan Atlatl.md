# Gungan Atlatl

A Gungan warrior could throw one of [their] signature plasma balls like a grenade (and often do), but a Gungan atlatl gives the weapon a greater range and aids in accuracy.  GUNGAN ATLATL Atlatls throw Gungan plasma balls, giving them greater range. An atlatl must have a supply of plasma balls for ammunition.
