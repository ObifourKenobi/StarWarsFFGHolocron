# N-4 Noise Grenade

Developed for crowd dispersal and riot control, Merr-Sonn's N-4 noise grenade emits a sharp blast of broad-spectrum sound, causing intense pain to those near the epicenter.  TRAPS AND TRIPWIRES As with frag grenades, a character can set  noise grenades  to detonate on impact, set them with a timer to detonate up to three rounds after being activated, or set up the explosive with connected a pressure detonator.
