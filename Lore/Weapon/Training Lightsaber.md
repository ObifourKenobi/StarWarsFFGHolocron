# Training Lightsaber

Ostensibly non-lethal variants of the standard lightsaber, training lightsabers are low-powered lightsabers used to train Jedi youngling ins lightsaber combat.  Models Include: None; each training lightsaber is individually crafted.  This lightsaber is a basic training lightsaber hilt containing an unmodded training lightsaber crystal. The crystal occupies two of the weapon's hard points. Lightsabers cannot be Sundered.
