# Staff of Office

Although worlds vary by custom, a great majority use historical or symbolic regalia and accessories to distinguish their diplomats, envoys, and other such luminaries from simple common folk.  STAFF OF OFFICE A staff of office requires two hands to wield. A character adds two boost dice when attempting to convincing others to allow [them] to retain [their] staff of office.
