WHAT IS IN THIS BOOK?

This book is an unofficial fan work, the product of a collaboration between many people that was not fully completed due to the aggressive initial scope and lack of long-term coordination. In its current state it is not intended to be a complete sourcebook for the First Order/Resistance era. Some content is not included because it already exists in official sources.
